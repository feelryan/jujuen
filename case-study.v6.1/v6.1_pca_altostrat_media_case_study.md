# Google Cloud
## PROFESSIONAL CLOUD ARCHITECT
## Altostrat Media Case Study

### Company Overview

Altostrat is a prominent player in the media industry, with an extensive collection of audio and video content that comprises podcasts, interviews, news broadcasts, and documentaries. Altostrat 是媒體產業中的知名企業，擁有涵蓋播客、訪談、新聞廣播和紀錄片等廣泛的音訊與視訊內容庫。 Their success in delivering premium content to a diverse audience requires a content management system that can keep pace with the dynamic media landscape. 他們成功地將優質內容傳遞給多元受眾，這需要一套能夠跟上動態媒體環境步伐的內容管理系統。

### Solution Concept

Altostrat seeks to modernize its content management and user engagement strategies using Google Cloud's generative AI. Altostrat 尋求利用 Google Cloud 的生成式 AI 來現代化其內容管理和使用者互動策略。 They want a platform that empowers customers with personalized recommendations, natural language interactions, and seamless self-service support. 他們想要一個能夠透過個人化推薦、自然語言互動和無縫自助服務支援來賦能客戶的平台。 Simultaneously, they want to drive revenue growth through dynamic pricing, targeted marketing, and personalized product suggestions. 同時，他們希望透過動態定價、精準行銷和個人化產品建議來推動營收成長。

The seamless integration of AI-powered tools into their existing Google Cloud environment will enable Altostrat to efficiently manage their vast media library, enhance user experiences, and unlock new revenue streams. 將 AI 驅動的工具無縫整合到現有的 Google Cloud 環境中，將使 Altostrat 能夠高效管理其龐大的媒體庫、增強使用者體驗並開啟新的營收來源。 Google Cloud's generative AI will solidify their leadership in the media industry. Google Cloud 的生成式 AI 將鞏固他們在媒體產業的領導地位。

### Existing Technical Environment

Altostrat's content management and delivery platform leverages GKE for scalability and high availability, essential for handling their vast media library. Altostrat 的內容管理和傳遞平台利用 GKE 來實現擴展性和高可用性，這對於處理其龐大的媒體庫至關重要。 Their extensive media library, spanning various documents, audio and video formats, is stored in Cloud Storage. 他們涵蓋各種文件、音訊和視訊格式的廣泛媒體庫儲存在 Cloud Storage 中。 To gain valuable insights into user behavior, content consumption patterns, and audience demographics, Altostrat leverages BigQuery as their primary data warehouse. 為了獲得關於使用者行為、內容消費模式和受眾特徵的寶貴洞察，Altostrat 利用 BigQuery 作為他們的主要資料倉儲。 Additionally, they use Cloud Run functions for serverless execution of event-driven tasks such as video transcoding, metadata extraction, and personalized content recommendations. 此外，他們使用 Cloud Run functions 來無伺服器執行事件驅動的任務，例如視訊轉碼、中繼資料擷取和個人化內容推薦。

***

**Google Cloud**

While Altostrat has made significant strides in cloud adoption, they also maintain some legacy on-premises systems for specific workflows like content ingestion and archival. 雖然 Altostrat 在雲端採用方面取得了重大進展，但他們仍保留了一些舊有的地端系統，用於內容攝取和歸檔等特定工作流程。 These systems are slated for modernization and migration to Google Cloud in the near future. 這些系統預計在不久的將來進行現代化並遷移至 Google Cloud。 User management and authentication are currently handled through a combination of Google Identity and third-party identity providers. 使用者管理和驗證目前是透過 Google Identity 和第三方身分識別提供者的組合來處理。 For monitoring and observability, Altostrat relies on a mix of native Google Cloud tools like Cloud Monitoring and open-source solutions like Prometheus, with alerts primarily delivered via email notifications. 在監控和可觀測性方面，Altostrat 依賴 Google Cloud 原生工具（如 Cloud Monitoring）和開源解決方案（如 Prometheus）的混合使用，警報主要透過電子郵件通知傳送。

### Business Requirements

*   Accelerate and enhance the reliability of operational workflows across all environments. [Google Cloud + On-premises]. 加速並增強所有環境中的營運工作流程可靠性。[Google Cloud + 地端]。
*   Simplify infrastructure management for rapid application deployment. 簡化基礎架構管理以實現快速應用程式部署。
*   Optimize cloud storage costs while maintaining high availability and scalability for media content. 在維持媒體內容的高可用性和擴展性的同時，最佳化雲端儲存成本。
*   Enable natural language interaction with the platform with 24/7 user support. 啟用與平台的自然語言互動，並提供 24/7 使用者支援。
*   Automatically generate concise summaries of media content. 自動產生簡潔的媒體內容摘要。
*   Extract rich metadata from media assets using NLP and computer vision. 使用 NLP 和電腦視覺從媒體資產中擷取豐富的中繼資料。
*   Detect and filter inappropriate content. 偵測並過濾不當內容。
*   Analyze media content to identify trends and extract insights. 分析媒體內容以識別趨勢並擷取洞察。
*   Inform content strategy and decision-making with data. 利用數據為內容策略和決策提供資訊。

### Technical Requirements

*   Modernize CI/CD for containerized deployments with a centralized management platform. 使用集中式管理平台現代化容器化部署的 CI/CD。
*   Secure, high-performance hybrid cloud connectivity for data ingestion. 用於資料攝取的安全、高效能混合雲連線。
*   Provide scalable, performant kubernetes environments both on-premises and in the cloud. 在地端和雲端提供可擴展、高效能的 Kubernetes 環境。
*   Optimize cloud storage costs for growing media volumes. 為不斷增長的媒體量最佳化雲端儲存成本。
*   Design AI-powered detection of harmful content. 設計 AI 驅動的有害內容偵測。
*   Ensure that AI systems are auditable and their decisions can be explained. 確保 AI 系統可被稽核，且其決策可以被解釋。
*   Leverage LLMs and conversational AI for personalized experiences and content virality. 利用大型語言模型 (LLMs) 和對話式 AI 來實現個人化體驗和內容病毒式傳播。
*   Develop advanced chatbots with natural language understanding to provide personalized assistance. 開發具備自然語言理解能力的高階聊天機器人，以提供個人化協助。
*   Automated summarization for diverse media. 多元媒體的自動摘要。
