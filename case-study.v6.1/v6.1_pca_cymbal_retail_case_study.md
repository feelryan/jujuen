# Google Cloud
**PROFESSIONAL CLOUD ARCHITECT**
**Cymbal Retail Case Study**

### Company Overview
**公司概覽**

Cymbal is an online retailer experiencing significant growth. Cymbal 是一家正在經歷顯著成長的線上零售商。 The retailer specializes in a large assortment of products spanning several retail sub-verticals, which makes managing their extensive product catalog a constant challenge. 該零售商專精於跨越多個零售子垂直領域的大量產品分類，這使得管理其龐大的產品目錄成為一項持續的挑戰。

### Solution Concept
**解決方案概念**

Cymbal wants to modernize its operations and enhance the customer experience in three core areas: Cymbal 希望在三個核心領域現代化其營運並增強客戶體驗：

*   **Catalog and Content Enrichment:** Cymbal wants to automate and improve the accuracy of their product catalog by utilizing gen AI to generate product attributes, descriptions, and images from supplier-provided information. **目錄與內容豐富化：** Cymbal 希望利用生成式 AI (gen AI) 根據供應商提供的資訊來生成產品屬性、描述和圖片，從而自動化並提高其產品目錄的準確性。 This solution will streamline their catalog management, reduce manual effort and errors, and ensure information is consistent across all their sales channels. 此解決方案將簡化其目錄管理，減少人工工作量和錯誤，並確保所有銷售管道中的資訊一致。

*   **Conversational Commerce with Product Discovery:** To enhance customer engagement and drive sales conversion, Cymbal wants to implement a Conversational Commerce solution. **具備產品發現功能的對話式商務：** 為了增強客戶互動並推動銷售轉換，Cymbal 希望實作一套對話式商務解決方案。 This solution will involve integrating AI-powered virtual agents into their website and mobile app to provide customers with a personalized and intuitive shopping experience through natural language conversations. 該解決方案將涉及將 AI 驅動的虛擬代理整合到其網站和行動應用程式中，透過自然語言對話為客戶提供個人化且直觀的購物體驗。 These agents will utilize Google Cloud's Discovery AI to process user requests and retrieve the most relevant products based on each customer's needs and preferences, creating a more engaging and satisfying shopping journey. 這些代理將利用 Google Cloud 的 Discovery AI 來處理使用者請求，並根據每位客戶的需求和偏好檢索最相關的產品，從而創造更具吸引力且令人滿意的購物旅程。

*   **Technical Stack Modernization:** To streamline operations and reduce costs around manual processes, data transfer, error handling and remediation, Cymbal wants to modernize their technical stack with cloud-based infrastructure, secure and efficient data handling, 3rd party integrations, and proactive monitoring and security. **技術堆疊現代化：** 為了簡化營運並降低圍繞人工流程、資料傳輸、錯誤處理和補救的成本，Cymbal 希望透過雲端基礎架構、安全高效的資料處理、第三方整合以及主動監控和安全性來現代化其技術堆疊。

# Google Cloud

### Existing Technical Environment
**現有技術環境**

Cymbal currently relies on the following environment: Cymbal 目前依賴以下環境：

*   A mix of on-premises and cloud-based systems. 混合了地端（on-premises）和雲端的系統。
*   A variety of databases, including MySQL, Microsoft SQL Server, Redis, and MongoDB, to store and manage its vast product catalog and customer data. 各種資料庫，包括 MySQL、Microsoft SQL Server、Redis 和 MongoDB，用於儲存和管理其龐大的產品目錄和客戶資料。
*   Kubernetes clusters to run containerized applications. 用於執行容器化應用程式的 Kubernetes 叢集。
*   Legacy file-based integrations with on-premises systems, including SFTP file transfers, ETL batch processing. 與地端系統的傳統檔案式整合，包括 SFTP 檔案傳輸和 ETL 批次處理。
*   A custom-built web application which allows customers to browse the product catalog by querying the relational databases for names and categories of products. 一個客製化建置的網頁應用程式，允許客戶透過查詢關聯式資料庫中的產品名稱和類別來瀏覽產品目錄。
*   An IVR (Interactive Voice Response) system to handle initial customer calls and route them to the appropriate departments or agents. 一個 IVR（互動式語音應答）系統，用於處理初始客戶來電並將其路由至適當的部門或專員。
*   Call center agents who receive transferred calls from the IVR system and manually enter orders into the system when a customer can't complete a transaction on their own. 客服中心專員從 IVR 系統接收轉接電話，並在客戶無法自行完成交易時手動將訂單輸入系統。
*   Various open source tools for monitoring such as Grafana, Nagios, and Elastic. 各種開源監控工具，例如 Grafana、Nagios 和 Elastic。

The current technical environment has encountered significant challenges: manual processes are time-consuming and error-prone, data silos limit a unified view of the customer journey, and integrating new technologies is difficult. 當前的技術環境遇到了重大挑戰：人工流程耗時且容易出錯，資料孤島（data silos）限制了對客戶旅程的統一視圖，且整合新技術相當困難。

### Business Requirements
**業務需求**

Cymbal has outlined these key business requirements for the gen AI solution: Cymbal 為生成式 AI 解決方案列出了這些關鍵業務需求：

*   **Automate Product Catalog Enrichment:** Reduce manual effort, minimize errors, and ensure accuracy and consistency across the product catalog. **自動化產品目錄豐富化：** 減少人工工作量，將錯誤降至最低，並確保產品目錄的準確性和一致性。
*   **Improve Product Discoverability:** Enhance search relevance and enable customers to find products more efficiently. **改善產品可發現性：** 增強搜尋相關性並使客戶能更有效率地找到產品。
*   **Increase Customer Engagement:** Create a more interactive and personalized shopping experience to improve customer satisfaction and potentially reduce product returns. **增加客戶互動：** 創造更具互動性和個人化的購物體驗，以提高客戶滿意度並可能減少產品退貨。
*   **Drive Sales Conversion:** Provide a more intuitive and helpful shopping experience to improve sales conversion rates and drive revenue growth. **推動銷售轉換：** 提供更直觀且更有幫助的購物體驗，以提高銷售轉換率並推動營收成長。
*   **Reduce costs:** Reduce call center staffing costs and data-center hosting costs. **降低成本：** 降低客服中心人員成本和資料中心託管成本。

# Google Cloud

### Technical Requirements
**技術需求**

*   **Attribute Generation:** Accurately derive relevant product attributes from various supplier data, including titles, descriptions, and images, ensuring the attributes align with the product category and Cymbal's existing catalog structure. **屬性生成：** 從各種供應商資料（包括標題、描述和圖片）中準確推導出相關的產品屬性，確保屬性與產品類別和 Cymbal 現有的目錄結構一致。
*   **Image Generation and Enhancement:** Generate different product image variations from a base image (e.g., showcasing various colors). **圖片生成與增強：** 從基礎圖片生成不同的產品圖片變體（例如：展示各種顏色）。 It should also support background changes, product color adjustments, and the addition of text overlays. 它還應支援背景更改、產品顏色調整以及添加文字疊加層。
*   **Automate Product Discovery:** Process customer requests expressed in natural language and return highly relevant product results. **自動化產品發現：** 處理以自然語言表達的客戶請求，並傳回高度相關的產品結果。
*   **Scalability and Performance:** The solution must handle Cymbal's extensive product catalog and accommodate their anticipated growth without compromising performance or user experience. **擴展性和效能：** 該解決方案必須能夠處理 Cymbal 龐大的產品目錄並適應其預期的成長，而不會犧牲效能或使用者體驗。
*   **Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) Review:** Provide a user interface (UI) for associates to review and manage gen AI-generated content, allowing them to approve, reject, or modify suggestions before updating the product catalog. **人機迴圈 (HITL) 審查：** 為員工提供使用者介面 (UI) 來審查和管理生成式 AI 產生的內容，允許他們在更新產品目錄之前批准、拒絕或修改建議。
*   **Data Security and Compliance:** Ensure all customer data, including product information and interactions with virtual agents, are handled securely and comply with relevant industry regulations. **資料安全與合規性：** 確保所有客戶資料（包括產品資訊以及與虛擬代理的互動）均得到安全處理，並符合相關產業法規。
