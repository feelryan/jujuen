# Google Cloud
**PROFESSIONAL CLOUD ARCHITECT**
**EHR Healthcare Case Study**
**專業雲端架構師 - EHR Healthcare 案例研究**

### Company Overview (公司概覽)

EHR Healthcare is a leading provider of electronic health record software to the medical industry. EHR Healthcare 是醫療產業中領先的電子病歷軟體供應商。 EHR Healthcare provides their software as a service to multi-national medical offices, hospitals, and insurance providers. EHR Healthcare 以軟體即服務 (SaaS) 的形式，向跨國醫療辦公室、醫院和保險供應商提供其軟體。

### Solution Concept (解決方案概念)

Due to rapid changes in the healthcare and insurance industry, EHR Healthcare's business has been growing exponentially year over year. 由於醫療保健和保險產業的快速變遷，EHR Healthcare 的業務逐年呈指數級成長。 They need to be able to scale their environment, adapt their disaster recovery plan, and roll out new continuous deployment capabilities to update their software at a fast pace. 他們需要能夠擴展其環境、調整其災難復原計畫，並推出新的持續部署功能，以快速更新其軟體。 Google Cloud has been chosen to replace their current colocation facilities. 已選定 Google Cloud 來取代他們目前的託管設施 (colocation facilities)。

### Existing Technical Environment (現有技術環境)

EHR's software is currently hosted in multiple colocation facilities. EHR 的軟體目前託管在多個託管設施中。 The lease on one of the data centers is about to expire. 其中一個資料中心的租約即將到期。

Customer-facing applications are web-based, and many have recently been containerized to run on a group of Kubernetes clusters. 面向客戶的應用程式是基於 Web 的，且許多最近已容器化，以便在 Kubernetes 叢集群組上運作。 Data is stored in a mixture of relational and NoSQL databases (MySQL, MS SQL Server, Redis, and MongoDB). 資料儲存在關聯式資料庫與 NoSQL 資料庫的混合環境中 (MySQL, MS SQL Server, Redis, 和 MongoDB)。

EHR is hosting several legacy file- and API-based integrations with insurance providers on-premises. EHR 正在地端 (on-premises) 託管數個與保險供應商進行的傳統檔案及 API 整合。 These systems are scheduled to be replaced over the next several years. 這些系統預計在未來幾年內被替換。 There is no plan to upgrade or move these systems at the current time. 目前沒有計畫升級或遷移這些系統。

Users are managed via Microsoft Active Directory. 使用者透過 Microsoft Active Directory 進行管理。 Monitoring is currently being done via various open source tools. 監控目前是透過各種開源工具進行的。 Alerts are sent via email and are often ignored. 警報透過電子郵件發送，且經常被忽略。

# Google Cloud

### Business Requirements (業務需求)

*   On-board new insurance providers as quickly as possible. 盡快讓新的保險供應商加入服務。
*   Provide a minimum 99.9% availability for all customer-facing systems. 為所有面向客戶的系統提供至少 99.9% 的可用性。
*   Provide centralized visibility and proactive action on system performance and usage. 提供對系統效能和使用情況的集中視覺化與主動應對措施。
*   Increase ability to provide insights into healthcare trends. 增強提供醫療保健趨勢洞察的能力。
*   Reduce latency to all customers. 降低對所有客戶的延遲。
*   Maintain regulatory compliance. 維持法規遵循。
*   Decrease infrastructure administration costs. 降低基礎架構管理成本。
*   Make predictions and generate reports on industry trends based on provider data. 根據供應商資料進行預測並產生產業趨勢報告。

### Technical Requirements (技術需求)

*   Maintain legacy interfaces to insurance providers with connectivity to both on-premises systems and cloud providers. 維護與保險供應商的傳統介面，並具備連接地端系統與雲端供應商的連線能力。
*   Provide a consistent way to manage customer-facing applications that are container-based. 提供一致的方式來管理基於容器的面向客戶應用程式。
*   Provide a secure and high-performance connection between on-premises systems and Google Cloud. 在地端系統與 Google Cloud 之間提供安全且高效能的連線。
*   Provide consistent logging, log retention, monitoring, and alerting capabilities. 提供一致的日誌記錄、日誌保留、監控與警報功能。
*   Maintain and manage multiple container-based environments. 維護並管理多個基於容器的環境。
*   Dynamically scale and provision new environments. 動態擴展並配置新環境。
*   Create interfaces to ingest and process data from new providers. 建立介面以攝取和處理來自新供應商的資料。

### Executive Statement (高層聲明)

Our on-premises strategy has worked for years but has required a major investment of time and money in training our team on distinctly different systems, managing similar but separate environments, and responding to outages. 我們的地端策略已運作多年，但需要在培訓團隊使用截然不同的系統、管理相似但分離的環境，以及應對服務中斷方面投入大量的時間與金錢。 Many of these outages have been a result of misconfigured systems, inadequate capacity to manage spikes in traffic, and inconsistent monitoring practices. 許多這類的中斷是由於系統配置錯誤、管理流量高峰的容量不足，以及不一致的監控實務所導致。 We want to use Google Cloud to leverage a scalable, resilient platform that can span multiple environments seamlessly and provide a consistent and stable user experience that positions us for future growth. 我們希望使用 Google Cloud 來利用可擴展且具備彈性的平台，該平台能夠無縫跨越多個環境，並提供一致且穩定的使用者體驗，為我們的未來成長奠定基礎。
