# KnightMotives Automotive Case Study
# Google Cloud 專業雲端架構師 KnightMotives 汽車案例研究

## Company Overview
## 公司概覽

KnightMotives is a car manufacturer specializing in autonomous, self-driving vehicles, including Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs), hybrids, and traditional internal combustion engine (ICE) vehicles. KnightMotives 是一家專門生產自動駕駛車輛的汽車製造商，產品包括電池電動車 (BEVs)、混合動力車和傳統內燃機 (ICE) 車輛。 While KnightMotives has made strides with the in-vehicle experience in their BEV fleet, the hybrid and ICE vehicles have yet to implement these new systems and are viewed poorly by critics and drivers. 雖然 KnightMotives 在其 BEV 車隊的車內體驗方面取得了長足進步，但混合動力車和 ICE 車輛尚未導入這些新系統，並且受到評論家和駕駛的負面評價。 The lack of modern in-vehicle technology in hybrid and ICE vehicles has resulted in declining sales and customer satisfaction. 混合動力車和 ICE 車輛缺乏現代化的車內技術，導致銷售量和客戶滿意度下降。

KnightMotives wants to modernize the consumer experience across all vehicles within five years. KnightMotives 希望在五年內全面現代化所有車輛的消費者體驗。 Artificial Intelligence offers a unique opportunity to revolutionize the in-vehicle experience, as well as the shopping, buying, and service/maintenance experience. 人工智慧提供了一個獨特的機會來徹底改革車內體驗，以及購物、購買和維修/保養體驗。 Investment in this new technology will require a shift in financial priorities on a global scale. 投資這項新技術將需要全球範圍內的財務優先順序轉變。

KnightMotives also wants to improve their online ordering system, which is unreliable. KnightMotives 也希望改善其不可靠的線上訂購系統。 Systems for customers to build their vehicle online for acquisition through a dealer are not delivering the data or reliability that dealers need, causing a strain in the relationship between KnightMotives and dealers. 讓客戶在線上打造車輛並透過經銷商取車的系統，無法提供經銷商所需的數據或可靠性，導致 KnightMotives 與經銷商之間的關係緊張。 Service technicians and sales staff need better tooling to enhance dealer successes, including built-to-order vehicles. 維修技術人員和銷售人員需要更好的工具來提升經銷商的成功率，包括接單生產 (built-to-order) 的車輛。

## Solution Concept
## 解決方案概念

KnightMotives wants to shift from manufacturing cars to creating a complete and compelling "automotive experience." KnightMotives 希望從製造汽車轉變為創造完整且引人入勝的「汽車體驗」。 Their strategy prioritizes delivering a consistent experience across all models, developing AI-powered features, generating new revenue from data monetization, adopting a digital focus to differentiate their brand from competitors, and developing better tools for mechanics and salespeople. 他們的策略優先考慮在所有車型中提供一致的體驗、開發 AI 驅動的功能、從數據變現中產生新收入、採用數位化重心以區別於競爭對手，並為機械師和銷售人員開發更好的工具。

## Existing Technical Environment
## 現有技術環境

KnightMotives's IT is largely on-premises with some applications on major cloud platforms. KnightMotives 的 IT 架構主要位於地端 (on-premises)，部分應用程式則位於主要雲端平台上。 Their supply chain runs on an outdated mainframe, and Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) is also outdated, making new promotions and dealer discounts difficult to implement. 他們的供應鏈運行在過時的大型主機上，且企業資源規劃 (ERP) 系統也已過時，導致難以實施新的促銷活動和經銷商折扣。 Dealers have no budget for new equipment. 經銷商沒有預算購買新設備。 There is fragmentation across vehicles with multiple code bases, and significant technical debt from supporting backwards compatibility. 車輛之間存在碎片化問題，擁有多個程式碼庫 (code bases)，且因支援向後相容性而產生大量技術債。 Network connectivity to manufacturing plants and vehicle connectivity in rural areas are challenges. 連接至製造工廠的網路連線以及偏遠地區的車輛連線能力皆是挑戰。

## Business Requirements
## 業務需求

Key business requirements include fostering a personalized relationship with the driver and delivering a cohesive experience across all models. 關鍵業務需求包括培養與駕駛者的個人化關係，並在所有車型中提供一致的體驗。 Creating a better build-to-order model will reduce time on the lot and provide transparency for both dealers and customers. 建立更好的接單生產模式將減少庫存時間，並為經銷商和客戶提供透明度。 Additionally, KnightMotives seeks to monetize corporate data to finance new technology investments, as their current AI infrastructure is obsolete and corporate data remains siloed. 此外，KnightMotives 尋求將企業數據變現以資助新技術投資，因為他們目前的 AI 基礎設施已經過時，且企業數據仍然處於孤島狀態。 Security is a paramount concern due to past data breaches. 由於過去的數據洩露事件，安全性是最重要的考量。 Adherence to European Union (EU) data protection regulations, especially for emerging autonomous platforms, is critical. 遵守歐盟 (EU) 數據保護法規，特別是針對新興的自動駕駛平台，至關重要。

KnightMotives plans to make significant investments in fully autonomous driving capabilities, with initial implementation targeting regions with favorable regulatory environments. KnightMotives 計劃在全自動駕駛能力方面進行重大投資，初步實施將針對監管環境有利的地區。 Prioritizing employee upskilling, attracting top-tier talent, and fostering better communication between business and technical teams are also critical objectives. 優先提升員工技能、吸引頂尖人才以及促進業務與技術團隊之間更好的溝通也是關鍵目標。

## Technical Requirements
## 技術需求

*   **Modernizing the in-vehicle experience** includes developing a consistent user experience (UX) that seamlessly integrates AI-powered features across all models, updating in-vehicle hardware and software in legacy models to support new UX features and AI capabilities, and ensuring reliable network connectivity, especially in rural areas, to support real-time AI features and data transmission. **現代化車內體驗** 包括開發一致的使用者體驗 (UX)，將 AI 驅動的功能無縫整合到所有車型中；更新舊車型的車內硬體和軟體以支援新的 UX 功能和 AI 能力；並確保可靠的網路連線（特別是在偏遠地區），以支援即時 AI 功能和數據傳輸。
*   **Network upgrades** are necessary to support increased data traffic and improve connectivity between plants and headquarters. **網路升級** 對於支援增加的數據流量並改善工廠與總部之間的連線是必要的。
*   **IT infrastructure modernization** requires adopting a hybrid cloud strategy to leverage the benefits of both on-premises and cloud infrastructure, and gradually modernizing or replacing legacy systems to improve efficiency and agility. **IT 基礎設施現代化** 需要採用混合雲策略，以利用地端和雲端基礎設施的優勢，並逐步現代化或汰換舊系統以提高效率和敏捷性。
*   **Autonomous vehicle development and testing** requires investing in cutting-edge AI and machine learning technologies, building a robust simulation environment, and ensuring compliance with evolving regulations related to autonomous vehicles. **自動駕駛車輛的開發與測試** 需要投資尖端的 AI 和機器學習技術，建立強大的模擬環境，並確保符合與自動駕駛車輛相關的不斷演變的法規。
*   **Data monetization and insights** requires implementing a robust data management platform, strict data security and privacy measures, and a scalable AI/ML infrastructure. **數據變現與洞察** 需要實施強大的數據管理平台、嚴格的數據安全與隱私措施，以及可擴展的 AI/ML 基礎設施。
*   **Increased focus on security and risk management** involves implementing a comprehensive security framework to protect against cyber threats and data breaches, developing an incident response plan, and providing security awareness training to employees. **加強對安全與風險管理的關注** 涉及實施全面的安全框架以防範網路威脅和數據洩露，制定事件回應計畫，並為員工提供安全意識培訓。
*   **Providing a delightful experience for dealers and customers** requires improving the online build-to-order system; developing modern dealer tools to streamline dealer operations, including sales, service, and inventory management; and implementing a comprehensive Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system to track customer interactions, personalize experiences, and improve customer satisfaction. **為經銷商和客戶提供愉快的體驗** 需要改善線上接單生產系統；開發現代化的經銷商工具以簡化經銷商營運，包括銷售、維修和庫存管理；並實施全面的客戶關係管理 (CRM) 系統來追蹤客戶互動、個人化體驗並提高客戶滿意度。

## 高層聲明

KnightMotives 致力於透過利用廣泛的數據（包含駕駛、路況、行為研究和碰撞安全統計數據）來提升安全性並拯救生命，為駕駛者創造引人入勝的數位體驗。 Our AI consistently outperforms national safety statistics, ensuring the unique and coveted KnightMotives experience is aligned across all our vehicle models. 我們的 AI 表現持續優於國家安全統計數據，確保所有車型都能提供獨特且令人嚮往的 KnightMotives 體驗。
