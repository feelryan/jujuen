## 3. 關鍵觀念與必備公式 (Key Concepts & Formulas)

### 觀念一：一元二次方程式的解法
### Concept 1: Methods for Solving Quadratic Equations

*   **定義**：形如 $ax^2 + bx + c = 0$ 的方程式。
    **Definition**: An equation in the form of $ax^2 + bx + c = 0$.
*   **必背技巧 (Essential Skills)**：
    1.  **十字交乘法 (Cross-multiplication)**：最快，優先使用。
        *   找兩個數相乘等於 $c$，相加等於 $b$。
        *   Find two numbers that multiply to $c$ and add up to $b$.
    2.  **配方法 (Completing the Square)**：
        *   口訣：「一半的平方」。將常數項移項，兩邊加上一次項係數一半的平方。
        *   Mnemonic: "Square of half." Move the constant term, then add the square of half the linear coefficient to both sides.
        *   公式：$(x+a)^2 = b$。
    3.  **判別式 (Discriminant)**：$D = b^2 - 4ac$。
        *   $D > 0$：兩相異實根 (Two distinct real roots).
        *   $D = 0$：重根 (Two equal real roots / Repeated root).
        *   $D < 0$：無解 (No real solution).

### 觀念二：三角形全等性質
### Concept 2: Triangle Congruence Properties

*   **直覺理解**：兩個三角形形狀、大小完全一樣，疊在一起會重合。
    **Intuitive understanding**: Two triangles are exactly the same in shape and size; they coincide when superimposed.
*   **判定性質 (Criteria)**：
    *   **SSS**：三邊對應相等 (Side-Side-Side).
    *   **SAS**：兩邊夾一角 (Side-Angle-Side).
    *   **ASA**：兩角夾一邊 (Angle-Side-Angle).
    *   **AAS**：兩角及其中一角的對邊 (Angle-Angle-Side).
    *   **RHS**：直角三角形，斜邊與一股 (Right angle-Hypotenuse-Side).
*   **注意**：**沒有** "SSA" 或 "AAA" 全等性質！
    **Note**: There is **NO** "SSA" or "AAA" congruence property!

### 觀念三：四邊形家族
### Concept 3: The Quadrilateral Family

*   **平行四邊形 (Parallelogram)**：
    *   對邊平行且相等 (Opposite sides are parallel and equal).
    *   對角相等 (Opposite angles are equal).
    *   **對角線互相平分** (Diagonals bisect each other).
*   **菱形 (Rhombus)**：
    *   四邊等長 (All four sides are equal).
    *   **對角線互相垂直平分** (Diagonals are perpendicular bisectors of each other).
*   **矩形/長方形 (Rectangle)**：
    *   四個角都是直角 (All four angles are right angles).
    *   **對角線等長且互相平分** (Diagonals are equal in length and bisect each other).

## 4. 典型題型整理 (Typical Question Patterns)

### 題型 A：幾何計算 - 角度與長度
### Pattern A: Geometric Calculation - Angles and Lengths

*   **題型說明**：題目給平行線或特定圖形（如菱形、摺紙），求未知角度或長度。
    **Description**: The problem gives parallel lines or specific shapes (like a rhombus or folded paper) and asks for unknown angles or lengths.
*   **解題步驟 (Steps)**：
    1.  **標示已知**：把題目給的數據（如 $\angle B = 112^\circ$）直接寫在圖上。
        **Mark the given**: Write the data given in the problem (e.g., $\angle B = 112^\circ$) directly on the diagram.
    2.  **找關係**：
        *   看到平行 $\rightarrow$ 找內錯角相等、同側內角互補。
        *   See parallel lines $\rightarrow$ look for equal alternate interior angles, supplementary consecutive interior angles.
        *   看到摺紙 $\rightarrow$ 摺疊前後的角度和邊長相等（全等）。
        *   See folded paper $\rightarrow$ angles and side lengths before and after folding are equal (congruent).
    3.  **列式計算**：利用三角形內角和 $180^\circ$ 或平角 $180^\circ$ 來計算。
        **Calculate**: Use the sum of triangle interior angles ($180^\circ$) or a straight angle ($180^\circ$) to calculate.

### 題型 B：代數應用 - 步道與面積問題
### Pattern B: Algebraic Application - Pathway and Area Problems

*   **題型說明**：一塊長方形土地，中間開闢道路或外圍加蓋步道，求路寬。
    **Description**: A rectangular plot of land where a road is built in the middle or a pathway is added around the outside; find the width of the road.
*   **解題步驟 (Steps)**：
    1.  **設未知數**：通常設路寬為 $x$。
        **Set the variable**: Usually let the road width be $x$.
    2.  **畫圖/平移**：如果是十字路口型的路，把路「推」到邊邊，讓剩下的土地變成一個完整的長方形。
        **Draw/Translate**: If it's a crossroad, "push" the roads to the edges so the remaining land becomes a single complete rectangle.
    3.  **列方程式**：(原本長 $\pm$ 變動) $\times$ (原本寬 $\pm$ 變動) $=$ 總面積。
        **Set up equation**: (Original Length $\pm$ Change) $\times$ (Original Width $\pm$ Change) $=$ Total Area.

## 5. 精選範例講解 (Worked Examples)

### 範例 1：摺紙問題 (幾何)
### Example 1: Folded Paper Problem (Geometry)

*(改編自試卷題型 / Adapted from exam questions)*

**題目**：
長方形紙條 $ABCD$ 沿對角線 $AC$ 對摺，點 $B$ 落在 $E$ 點，$\overline{AE}$ 與 $\overline{DC}$ 交於 $P$ 點。若 $\angle ACB = 25^\circ$，求 $\angle APC$ 的度數。
**Problem**:
A rectangular strip $ABCD$ is folded along the diagonal $AC$ so that point $B$ falls on point $E$, and $\overline{AE}$ intersects $\overline{DC}$ at point $P$. If $\angle ACB = 25^\circ$, find the degree of $\angle APC$.

**解題思路 (Thought Process)**：
直覺上，摺紙就是「全等」，所以摺過去的角跟原來的角一樣大。
Intuitively, folding paper means "congruence," so the folded angle is the same size as the original angle.

**詳細步驟 (Detailed Steps)**：

1.  **利用平行線性質**：
    因為長方形對邊平行 ($\overline{AB} // \overline{CD}$)，所以內錯角相等。
    **Use parallel line properties**:
    Since opposite sides of a rectangle are parallel ($\overline{AB} // \overline{CD}$), alternate interior angles are equal.
    $\angle BAC = \angle ACD = \angle ACB$ (等等，長方形對角線不一定平分角，這裡要小心)。
    Wait, diagonals of a rectangle don't necessarily bisect the angle. Let's correct this:
    $\angle BAC$ (內錯角 alternate interior angle) $= \angle ACD$ (錯了，是 $\angle P C A$).
    正確是：$\overline{AB} // \overline{DC} \implies \angle BAC = \angle P C A$。
    Correctly: $\overline{AB} // \overline{DC} \implies \angle BAC = \angle P C A$.
    *但題目給的是 $\angle ACB = 25^\circ$。*
    *But the problem gives $\angle ACB = 25^\circ$.*
    在矩形中 $\angle B = 90^\circ$，所以 $\angle BAC = 90^\circ - 25^\circ = 65^\circ$。
    In a rectangle $\angle B = 90^\circ$, so $\angle BAC = 90^\circ - 25^\circ = 65^\circ$.

2.  **利用摺疊性質 (全等)**：
    摺疊後 $\triangle ABC \cong \triangle AEC$。
    **Use folding property (Congruence)**:
    After folding, $\triangle ABC \cong \triangle AEC$.
    所以 $\angle EAC = \angle BAC = 65^\circ$。
    Therefore, $\angle EAC = \angle BAC = 65^\circ$.

3.  **計算外角**：
    觀察 $\triangle APC$，外角 $\angle APC$ ... 不對，$\angle APC$ 是鈍角。
    Observe $\triangle APC$. The exterior angle... no, $\angle APC$ is an obtuse angle.
    讓我們看 $\triangle PAC$。
    Let's look at $\triangle PAC$.
    $\angle PAC = \angle EAC = 65^\circ$。
    $\angle PCA = \angle ACB = 25^\circ$ (因為原圖是矩形，對邊平行，內錯角相等... 不對，$\angle PCA$ 就是原來的 $\angle ACD$ 的一部分，這裡不需要內錯角，直接看圖)。
    *修正思路 (Corrected logic)*:
    $\angle P C A$ 其實就是 $\angle A C D$。因為 $\overline{AB} // \overline{CD}$，所以 $\angle BAC = \angle ACD = 65^\circ$。
    $\angle P C A$ is actually $\angle A C D$. Since $\overline{AB} // \overline{CD}$, $\angle BAC = \angle ACD = 65^\circ$.
    
    現在我們有 $\triangle PAC$ 的兩個角：
    Now we have two angles of $\triangle PAC$:
    $\angle PAC = 65^\circ$ (來自摺疊 from folding).
    $\angle PCA = 65^\circ$ (來自平行內錯角 from parallel alternate interior angles).
    
    $\angle APC = 180^\circ - 65^\circ - 65^\circ = 50^\circ$。
    $\angle APC = 180^\circ - 65^\circ - 65^\circ = 50^\circ$.
    
    *(自我檢查：等腰三角形？是的，因為底角相等。)*
    *(Self-check: Is it an isosceles triangle? Yes, because base angles are equal.)*

### 範例 2：步道寬度問題 (代數)
### Example 2: Pathway Width Problem (Algebra)

*(參考 PDF 第 2 頁題 2 / Based on PDF Page 2, Q2)*

**題目**：
一塊長 20 公尺、寬 18 公尺的空地。中間蓋大樓，外圍留一圈等寬的步道。已知大樓面積是整塊空地的 60%。求步道寬度？
**Problem**:
An empty lot is 20 meters long and 18 meters wide. A building is constructed in the center, leaving a pathway of uniform width around the outside. The building area is 60% of the total lot area. Find the width of the pathway.

**解題思路 (Thought Process)**：
大樓在中間，步道在四周。如果設步道寬為 $x$，大樓的長和寬都會被「扣掉」兩個 $x$ (左邊一個，右邊一個)。
The building is in the middle, pathway around it. If we let the pathway width be $x$, both the length and width of the building will have two $x$'s "subtracted" (one on the left, one on the right).

**詳細步驟 (Detailed Steps)**：

1.  **設未知數**：
    設步道寬度為 $x$ 公尺。
    **Set the variable**:
    Let the width of the pathway be $x$ meters.

2.  **表示大樓的長與寬**：
    大樓長 $= 20 - 2x$。
    Building Length $= 20 - 2x$.
    大樓寬 $= 18 - 2x$。
    Building Width $= 18 - 2x$.

3.  **列出方程式**：
    大樓面積 $=$ 總面積 $\times 60\%$。
    Building Area $=$ Total Area $\times 60\%$.
    $(20 - 2x)(18 - 2x) = (20 \times 18) \times 0.6$。
    $(20 - 2x)(18 - 2x) = 360 \times 0.6 = 216$.

4.  **化簡與求解**：
    展開：$360 - 40x - 36x + 4x^2 = 216$。
    Expand: $360 - 76x + 4x^2 = 216$.
    移項整理：$4x^2 - 76x + 144 = 0$。
    Rearrange: $4x^2 - 76x + 144 = 0$.
    同除以 4：$x^2 - 19x + 36 = 0$。
    Divide by 4: $x^2 - 19x + 36 = 0$.
    因式分解：$(x - ?)(x - ?) = 0$ ... 找不到整數？
    Factor: $(x - ?)(x - ?) = 0$ ... Can't find integers?
    檢查題目是否需要公式解。題目給了 $\sqrt{217}$ 的提示，表示這題不能十字交乘！
    Check if the problem requires the quadratic formula. The problem gave a hint for $\sqrt{217}$, meaning this cannot be solved by cross-multiplication!
    
    使用公式解：$x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$
    Use Quadratic Formula: $x = \frac{19 \pm \sqrt{(-19)^2 - 4(1)(36)}}{2}$
    $x = \frac{19 \pm \sqrt{361 - 144}}{2} = \frac{19 \pm \sqrt{217}}{2}$
    
    利用題目提示 $\sqrt{217} \approx 14.73$。
    Use the hint $\sqrt{217} \approx 14.73$.
    $x \approx \frac{19 \pm 14.73}{2}$。
    
    案 1：$x \approx \frac{33.73}{2} \approx 16.8$ (不合，因為寬只有 18，扣掉兩個 16.8 就變負的了)。
    Case 1: $x \approx 16.8$ (Invalid, because width is only 18; subtracting two 16.8s makes it negative).
    案 2：$x \approx \frac{4.27}{2} \approx 2.1$。
    Case 2: $x \approx 2.1$.

5.  **答案**：步道寬約 2.1 公尺。
    **Answer**: The pathway is approximately 2.1 meters wide.

## 6. 常見錯誤與迷思 (Common Mistakes & Misconceptions)

1.  **忘記有兩個根**
    **Forgetting there are two roots**
    *   錯誤：解 $x^2 = 36$，寫 $x = 6$。
    *   Error: Solving $x^2 = 36$, writing $x = 6$.
    *   修正：記得正負號，$x = \pm 6$。
    *   Correction: Remember the plus-minus sign, $x = \pm 6$.

2.  **應用題忘記檢查「不合」的解**
    **Forgetting to check for "invalid" solutions in word problems**
    *   錯誤：算出邊長是 $5$ 或 $-3$，答案全寫。
    *   Error: Calculating side length as $5$ or $-3$, and writing both as the answer.
    *   修正：邊長、時間、個數不能是負的，要寫「(負不合)」。
    *   Correction: Length, time, and quantity cannot be negative; write "(negative is invalid)".

3.  **全等性質亂用**
    **Misusing congruence properties**
    *   錯誤：看到兩個邊和一個角相等，就說是全等 (SSA)。
    *   Error: Seeing two sides and one angle equal, and claiming congruence (SSA).
    *   修正：只有 RHS (直角) 可以用「邊邊角」，其他必須是 SAS (夾角)。
    *   Correction: Only RHS (right angle) allows "Side-Side-Angle"; others must be SAS (included angle).

4.  **統計表看錯欄位**
    **Reading the wrong column in statistical tables**
    *   錯誤：題目問「累積次數」，你卻看成「相對次數」。
    *   Error: The question asks for "Cumulative Frequency," but you look at "Relative Frequency."
    *   口訣：看到「以上/以下」或「累積」字樣，就是看最後一直加總的那一欄。
    *   Mnemonic: If you see "more than/less than" or "cumulative," look at the column that sums up everything.
