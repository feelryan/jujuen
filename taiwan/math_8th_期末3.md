    **Crack long word problems** by converting text descriptions into mathematical equations.

## 3. 關鍵觀念與必備公式 (Key Concepts & Formulas)

### **觀念一：一元二次方程式的解法 (Methods to Solve Quadratic Equations)**

**定義與直覺理解：**
目標是找出讓等式成立的 $x$ 值。想像你在找拋物線與地面（x軸）的交點。
**Definition and Intuitive Understanding:**
The goal is to find the $x$ values that make the equation true. Imagine you are finding where a parabola crosses the ground (the x-axis).

**必背公式 (Must-Memorize Formulas):**

1.  **配方法 (Completing the Square):**
    把方程式整理成 $(x+a)^2 = b$ 的樣子。
    Rearrange the equation into the form $(x+a)^2 = b$.
    *   口訣：加上「一次項係數一半的平方」。
    *   Mnemonic: Add "the square of half the coefficient of the linear term".

2.  **公式解 (Quadratic Formula):**
    若 $ax^2 + bx + c = 0$，則：
    If $ax^2 + bx + c = 0$, then:
    $$x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$$

3.  **判別式 (Discriminant):** $D = b^2 - 4ac$
    *   $D > 0$: 相異兩根 (Two distinct roots)
    *   $D = 0$: 重根 (Repeated root / One solution)
    *   $D < 0$: 無解 (No real solution)

**何時使用？ (When to use?)**
*   看到數字簡單、好分解 $\rightarrow$ **十字交乘 (Cross-multiplication)**。
*   題目問「配方過程」或「完全平方式」 $\rightarrow$ **配方法 (Completing the Square)**。
*   數字很醜、無法分解 $\rightarrow$ **公式解 (Quadratic Formula)**。

---

### **觀念二：統計圖表的解讀 (Reading Statistical Charts)**

**定義與直覺理解：**
統計圖就是把數據「畫」出來。重點在於區分「這組有多少人」和「這組佔全部的幾%」。
**Definition and Intuitive Understanding:**
Statistical charts are just visualizing data. The key is distinguishing between "how many people in this group" and "what percentage of the total this group represents".

**必背符號 (Symbols):**
*   **相對次數 (Relative Frequency):** $\frac{\text{該組次數 (Group Count)}}{\text{總次數 (Total Count)}} \times 100\%$
*   **累積次數 (Cumulative Frequency):** 從第一組一直加到目前這一組的總和。
    The sum from the first group up to the current group.

**常見題型情境：**
*   給折線圖，問你「不及格的人有多少？」或「中位數落在哪一組？」。
    Given a line graph, asking "How many people failed?" or "Which group contains the median?".

---

### **觀念三：平行線與四邊形 (Parallel Lines & Quadrilaterals)**

**必背性質 (Key Properties):**
1.  **平行線 (Parallel Lines):**
    *   同位角相等 (Corresponding angles are equal).
    *   內錯角相等 (Alternate interior angles are equal).
    *   同側內角互補 (Consecutive interior angles are supplementary / add up to 180°).

2.  **平行四邊形 (Parallelogram):**
    *   對邊等長 (Opposite sides are equal).
    *   對角相等 (Opposite angles are equal).
    *   對角線互相平分 (Diagonals bisect each other).

## 4. 典型題型整理 (Typical Question Patterns)

### **題型 A：配方法的步驟除錯 (Error Analysis in Completing the Square)**
*(常見於三民、新興試卷 / Seen in Sanmin & Xinxing papers)*

**題型說明：**
題目會列出解題的 Step 1 到 Step 4，問你哪一步開始錯。
**Question Description:**
The problem lists Step 1 to Step 4 of a solution and asks where the error started.

**解題步驟 (Steps):**
1.  **檢查移項 (Check transposition):** 常數項移到右邊變號了嗎？
    Did the constant term change sign when moved to the right?
2.  **檢查係數 (Check coefficient):** 二次項係數 $x^2$ 必須是 1。如果不是，全式要除以 $a$。
    The coefficient of the $x^2$ term must be 1. If not, divide the whole equation by $a$.
3.  **檢查配方數 (Check the added term):** 等號兩邊是否都加上了 $(\frac{b}{2})^2$？
    Did both sides of the equation add $(\frac{b}{2})^2$?

**小提示 (Tip):**
學生最常忘記「等號右邊也要加數字」。
Students most often forget to "add the number to the right side of the equation as well".

---

### **題型 B：應用問題 - 道路與面積 (Word Problems - Roads & Area)**
*(常見於各校試卷 / Seen in various papers)*

**題型說明：**
一塊長方形土地，中間開闢十字形或等寬的道路，求剩餘面積或道路寬度。
**Question Description:**
A rectangular field with cross-shaped or uniform-width roads cut through it. Find the remaining area or the width of the road.

**解題步驟 (Steps):**
1.  **直覺平移 (Intuitive Translation):** 不要把路切碎。想像把道路全部推到邊邊。
    Don't chop up the road. Imagine pushing all the roads to the edges.
2.  **列式 (Set up equation):**
    $(\text{原長} - x)(\text{原寬} - x) = \text{剩餘面積}$
    $(\text{Original Length} - x)(\text{Original Width} - x) = \text{Remaining Area}$
3.  **求解 (Solve):** 解出 $x$ 後，記得檢查是否合理（路寬不能比地還寬）。
    After solving for $x$, remember to check if it makes sense (road width cannot be wider than the field).

## 5. 精選範例講解 (Worked Examples)

### **範例 1：一元二次方程式的解 (Solving Quadratic Equations)**
*(改編自中山國中試題 / Adapted from Zhongshan JH Exam)*

**題目 (Problem):**
若 $x$ 的一元二次方程式 $x^2 - 4x - 396 = 0$ 的兩根為 $a, b$，且 $a > b$，求 $a - b$ 之值。
If the two roots of the quadratic equation $x^2 - 4x - 396 = 0$ are $a$ and $b$, with $a > b$, find the value of $a - b$.

**解題思路 (Thought Process):**
*   **直覺 (Intuition):** 數字 396 很大，十字交乘很難湊。
    The number 396 is large, so cross-multiplication is hard to guess.
*   **策略 (Strategy):** 用「配方法」或「公式解」會比較快。這裡示範配方法，因為 $x$ 係數是偶數。
    Using "Completing the Square" or "Quadratic Formula" is faster. I will demonstrate Completing the Square because the coefficient of $x$ is even.

**詳細解答 (Solution):**

1.  **移項 (Move constant):**
    $x^2 - 4x = 396$
2.  **配方 (Complete the square):** 加上 $(\frac{4}{2})^2 = 4$。
    Add $(\frac{4}{2})^2 = 4$.
    $x^2 - 4x + 4 = 396 + 4$
3.  **整理 (Simplify):**
    $(x - 2)^2 = 400$
4.  **開根號 (Take square root):**
    $x - 2 = \pm 20$
    *(注意：400 是 20 的平方 / Note: 400 is the square of 20)*
5.  **求解 (Solve for x):**
    $x = 2 \pm 20$
    $x = 22$ 或 (or) $x = -18$
6.  **回答問題 (Answer):**
    $a = 22, b = -18$
    $a - b = 22 - (-18) = 40$

### **範例 2：幾何摺紙問題 (Geometry Folding Problem)**
*(改編自仁愛國中試題 / Adapted from Renai JH Exam)*

**題目 (Problem):**
將長方形紙片沿對角線摺疊，若 $\angle 1 = 40^\circ$，求摺疊後形成的 $\angle 2$ 是幾度？
Fold a rectangular paper along the diagonal. If $\angle 1 = 40^\circ$, what is the measure of the resulting $\angle 2$?

**解題思路 (Thought Process):**
*   **直覺 (Intuition):** 摺紙問題最重要的是「摺過去的角跟原本的角一樣大」。
    The most important thing in folding problems is that "the folded angle is equal to the original angle".
*   **關鍵 (Key):** 平行線會有「內錯角相等」。
    Parallel lines have "equal alternate interior angles".

**詳細解答 (Solution):**

1.  **利用內錯角 (Use Alternate Interior Angles):**
    因為長方形上下邊平行，所以對角線造成的內錯角相等。
    Since the top and bottom sides of a rectangle are parallel, the alternate interior angles created by the diagonal are equal.
    $\rightarrow$ 摺痕旁邊的角也是 $40^\circ$ (The angle next to the fold is also $40^\circ$).
2.  **利用摺疊性質 (Use Folding Property):**
    摺過去的角 = 原本的角。
    Folded angle = Original angle.
    $\rightarrow$ 所以重疊部分的頂角是 $40^\circ$ (So the vertex angle of the overlapping part is $40^\circ$).
3.  **計算平角 (Calculate Straight Angle):**
    這三個角（原角、摺角、剩下的角）在同一條直線上嗎？通常題目是問三角形的外角或底角。
    *(此類題目通常形成等腰三角形 / This type of problem usually forms an isosceles triangle)*.
    若形成等腰三角形，底角為 $40^\circ$，則頂角 $= 180 - 40 - 40 = 100^\circ$。
    If it forms an isosceles triangle with base angles of $40^\circ$, the vertex angle is $180 - 40 - 40 = 100^\circ$.

*(註：具體數字需視題目圖形而定，但記住口訣：**摺紙必有角平分線與等腰三角形**)*
*(Note: Specific numbers depend on the diagram, but remember the mantra: **Folding always creates angle bisectors and isosceles triangles**)*

## 6. 常見錯誤與迷思 (Common Mistakes & Misconceptions)

1.  **公式解忘記除以 2a (Forgetting to divide by 2a in Quadratic Formula)**
    *   **錯誤 (Mistake):** $x = -b \pm \sqrt{b^2-4ac}$
    *   **修正 (Correction):** 分母是 $2a$，不要漏掉！
        The denominator is $2a$, don't leave it out!

2.  **判別式計算錯誤 (Calculation error in Discriminant)**
    *   **錯誤 (Mistake):** 在算 $b^2 - 4ac$ 時，如果 $c$ 是負數，常忘記「負負得正」。
        When calculating $b^2 - 4ac$, if $c$ is negative, students often forget that "negative times negative equals positive".
    *   **檢查 (Check):** 寫下來：$-4 \times (a) \times (-c) = + \text{正數}$。

3.  **統計圖表看錯軸 (Reading the wrong axis in Statistics)**
    *   **錯誤 (Mistake):** 把「累積次數」看成「相對次數」。
        Confusing "Cumulative Frequency" with "Relative Frequency".
    *   **檢查 (Check):** 看到折線圖尾端是 100 或 總人數，那就是「累積」。
        If the end of the line graph hits 100 or the Total Count, it is "Cumulative".

4.  **幾何性質混淆 (Confusing Geometry Properties)**
    *   **錯誤 (Mistake):** 以為「菱形」的對角線會相等。
        Thinking that the diagonals of a "Rhombus" are equal.
    *   **修正 (Correction):** 菱形對角線是「互相垂直」，矩形（長方形）對角線才是「相等」。
        Rhombus diagonals are "perpendicular"; Rectangle diagonals are "equal".

## 7. 建議練習與自我檢核 (Practice Suggestions & Self-Check)

**練習建議 (Practice Suggestions):**
1.  **先做計算題 (Do calculation problems first):** 確保配方法和公式解 100% 正確，這是基本分。
2.  **畫圖 (Draw diagrams):** 幾何題不要只看題目，一定要在紙上畫出平行線、標上角度。
3.  **限時練習 (Timed practice):** 挑一份試卷，設定 45 分鐘寫完，模擬考試壓力。

**考前自我檢核表 (Pre-Exam Self-Checklist):**

*   [ ] 我能默寫出一元二次方程式的公式解。
    I can write down the quadratic formula from memory.
*   [ ] 我知道 $b^2-4ac < 0$ 代表無解。
    I know that $b^2-4ac < 0$ means no real solution.
*   [ ] 我能從累積相對次數折線圖中找出中位數（第 50% 的位置）。
    I can find the median (the 50% position) from a cumulative relative frequency line graph.
*   [ ] (若考幾何) 我知道平行四邊形的 3 個判別性質。
    (If testing Geometry) I know the 3 criteria for identifying a parallelogram.
