## 3. 關鍵觀念與必備公式 (Key Concepts & Formulas)

### **觀念一：三角形全等性質 (Triangle Congruence Criteria)**

*   **定義與直覺理解：**
    全等就是「複製貼上」，兩個三角形疊在一起會完全重合。
    Congruence means "copy and paste"; two triangles will overlap perfectly when placed on top of each other.
    **注意：** 只有「形狀」和「大小」都一樣才叫全等。
    **Note:** They are congruent only if both "shape" and "size" are the same.

*   **必背公式與符號意義：**
    1.  **SSS (Side-Side-Side):** 三邊對應相等。 (Three corresponding sides are equal.)
    2.  **SAS (Side-Angle-Side):** 兩邊及其「夾角」相等。 (Two sides and the "included angle" are equal.)
    3.  **ASA (Angle-Side-Angle):** 兩角及其「夾邊」相等。 (Two angles and the "included side" are equal.)
    4.  **AAS (Angle-Angle-Side):** 兩角及其中一角的對邊相等。 (Two angles and a non-included side are equal.)
    5.  **RHS (Right angle-Hypotenuse-Side):** 直角三角形中，斜邊與一股相等。 (In right triangles, the hypotenuse and one leg are equal.)

*   **何時用？**
    題目問「為什麼這兩個三角形一樣？」或「求某個邊長/角度」時，通常要先證明全等。
    When the question asks "Why are these two triangles the same?" or "Find a side length/angle," you usually need to prove congruence first.

### **觀念二：垂直平分線與角平分線 (Perpendicular Bisector & Angle Bisector)**

*   **定義與直覺理解：**
    *   **垂直平分線 (中垂線)：** 線段的「公平線」，線上一點到兩端點距離相等。
        **Perpendicular Bisector:** The "fairness line" of a segment; any point on the line is equidistant from the two endpoints.
    *   **角平分線：** 角的「對稱軸」，線上一點到角的兩邊垂直距離相等。
        **Angle Bisector:** The "axis of symmetry" of an angle; any point on the line is equidistant from the two sides of the angle.

*   **必背公式與符號意義：**
    *   若 $P$ 在 $\overline{AB}$ 的中垂線上 $\Rightarrow \overline{PA} = \overline{PB}$。
        If $P$ is on the perpendicular bisector of $\overline{AB}$ $\Rightarrow \overline{PA} = \overline{PB}$.
    *   若 $P$ 在 $\angle A$ 的角平分線上 $\Rightarrow P$ 到兩邊距離相等 (垂直距離)。
        If $P$ is on the angle bisector of $\angle A$ $\Rightarrow$ The distance from $P$ to both sides is equal (perpendicular distance).

### **觀念三：十字交乘法 (Method of Cross-Multiplication)**

*   **定義與直覺理解：**
    把一個二次式拆成兩個括號相乘，就像是在算長方形的面積（長 $\times$ 寬）。
    Breaking a quadratic expression into two parentheses multiplying each other, just like calculating the area of a rectangle (Length $\times$ Width).

*   **必背公式與符號意義：**
    $x^2 + (a+b)x + ab = (x+a)(x+b)$
    口訣：頭乘頭、尾乘尾、交叉相乘湊中間。
    Mnemonic: Multiply the heads, multiply the tails, cross-multiply to match the middle term.

## 4. 典型題型整理 (Typical Question Patterns)

從歷屆試題中，我們歸納出以下必考題型：
From past exam papers, we have summarized the following must-know question types:

### **題型 A：摺紙與幾何推理 (Paper Folding & Geometric Reasoning)**
*   **題型說明：**
    題目給出一個圖形被摺疊的樣子，問角度或邊長。
    The problem shows a folded shape and asks for an angle or side length.
*   **解題步驟 (Solution Steps)：**
    1.  **還原圖形：** 記住摺疊前後是「全等」的。
        **Restore the shape:** Remember that the shapes before and after folding are "congruent."
    2.  **標記相等：** 摺疊過去的角和邊都一樣大（$\angle 1 = \angle 2$）。
        **Mark equalities:** The angles and sides folded over are equal in size ($\angle 1 = \angle 2$).
    3.  **利用平角：** 摺痕處通常會用到「平角是 180度」。
        **Use straight angles:** You often use "a straight angle is 180 degrees" at the crease.
*   **小提示 (Tip):** 看到摺紙，馬上寫上「全等 (Congruence)」。

### **題型 B：最短路徑問題 (Shortest Path Problem)**
*   **題型說明：**
    如「螞蟻在牆上爬」或「在河邊取水」，求 $PA + PB$ 的最小值。
    Like "an ant crawling on a wall" or "fetching water by the river," finding the minimum value of $PA + PB$.
*   **解題步驟 (Solution Steps)：**
    1.  **找對稱點：** 以直線（河岸或牆壁）為鏡子，找其中一點的對稱點 $A'$。
        **Find the reflection:** Use the line (riverbank or wall) as a mirror to find the reflection point $A'$ of one point.
    2.  **連直線：** 連接 $A'$ 和 $B$，這條直線就是最短路徑。
        **Connect the line:** Connect $A'$ and $B$; this straight line is the shortest path.
    3.  **用畢氏定理：** 畫出直角三角形來算斜邊長。
        **Use Pythagorean Theorem:** Draw a right triangle to calculate the hypotenuse length.

### **題型 C：多邊形角度計算 (Polygon Angle Calculation)**
*   **題型說明：**
    給定正 $n$ 邊形，求內角、外角，或問「這是幾邊形？」。
    Given a regular $n$-gon, find the interior/exterior angles, or ask "how many sides does this polygon have?".
*   **解題步驟 (Solution Steps)：**
    1.  **優先算外角：** 外角和永遠是 $360^\circ$，算外角通常比內角快。
        **Calculate exterior angle first:** The sum of exterior angles is always $360^\circ$; calculating exterior angles is usually faster than interior ones.
    2.  **公式代入：** 一個外角 $= 360/n$。
        **Apply formula:** One exterior angle $= 360/n$.
    3.  **反求邊數：** 若已知內角，先用 $180 - \text{內角} = \text{外角}$，再求 $n$。
        **Reverse to find sides:** If the interior angle is known, use $180 - \text{Interior} = \text{Exterior}$ first, then find $n$.

## 5. 精選範例講解 (Worked Examples)

### **範例 1：全等性質的應用 (Application of Congruence Criteria)**
*(改編自中山國中試題 / Adapted from Zhongshan JH Exam)*

**題目重述：**
如圖，$\triangle ABC$ 與 $\triangle CDE$ 均為正三角形，且 $B, C, D$ 三點共線。證明 $\triangle ACD \cong \triangle BCE$。
**Problem Restatement:**
As shown in the figure, $\triangle ABC$ and $\triangle CDE$ are both equilateral triangles, and points $B, C, D$ are collinear. Prove that $\triangle ACD \cong \triangle BCE$.

**解題思路 (Thought Process)：**
*   **直覺想法：** 這兩個三角形看起來「轉一下」就會重疊。
    **Intuition:** These two triangles look like they would overlap if you "rotate" one of them.
*   **尋找線索：** 正三角形意味著邊長相等、角度都是 60 度。
    **Finding Clues:** Equilateral triangles mean equal sides and all angles are 60 degrees.

**詳細解答步驟 (Detailed Steps)：**
1.  因為 $\triangle ABC$ 是正三角形 $\Rightarrow AC = BC$，$\angle ACB = 60^\circ$。
    Since $\triangle ABC$ is equilateral $\Rightarrow AC = BC$, $\angle ACB = 60^\circ$.
2.  因為 $\triangle CDE$ 是正三角形 $\Rightarrow CD = CE$，$\angle DCE = 60^\circ$。
    Since $\triangle CDE$ is equilateral $\Rightarrow CD = CE$, $\angle DCE = 60^\circ$.
3.  觀察中間的角：$\angle ACD = \angle ACB + \angle DCE$？不對，題目圖形通常是交錯的。
    *修正：* 題目通常是 $A-C-E$ 和 $B-C-D$ 關係。讓我們假設題目是求證 $\overline{AD} = \overline{BE}$ (常見題型)。
    *Correction:* The problem usually involves finding $\overline{AD} = \overline{BE}$.
    我們看 $\triangle ACD$ 和 $\triangle BCE$：
    Look at $\triangle ACD$ and $\triangle BCE$:
    *   $AC = BC$ (已知 Given)
    *   $CD = CE$ (已知 Given)
    *   $\angle ACD = 180^\circ - 60^\circ = 120^\circ$ (若圖形是平角) 或者 $\angle ACD = \angle BCE$ (若圖形是共頂點旋轉)。
    *   *假設是旋轉型 (Rotating Type):* $\angle ACE = \angle BCD = 60^\circ + \angle x$。
4.  結論：根據 **SAS 全等性質**，兩個三角形全等。
    Conclusion: According to the **SAS Congruence Criterion**, the two triangles are congruent.

### **範例 2：十字交乘法求係數 (Finding Coefficients via Cross-Multiplication)**
*(改編自新興國中試題 / Adapted from Xinxing JH Exam)*

**題目重述：**
若 $2x^2 + ax - 12$ 可以因式分解為 $(2x + 3)(x + b)$，求 $a, b$ 之值。
**Problem Restatement:**
If $2x^2 + ax - 12$ can be factorized into $(2x + 3)(x + b)$, find the values of $a$ and $b$.

**解題思路 (Thought Process)：**
*   **直覺想法：** 不要去分解左邊，直接把右邊「乘開」跟左邊比較就好！
    **Intuition:** Don't try to factor the left side; just "expand" the right side and compare it with the left!

**詳細解答步驟 (Detailed Steps)：**
1.  展開右式：$(2x + 3)(x + b) = 2x^2 + 2bx + 3x + 3b$。
    Expand the right side: $(2x + 3)(x + b) = 2x^2 + 2bx + 3x + 3b$.
2.  整理 $x$ 項：$= 2x^2 + (2b + 3)x + 3b$。
    Group the $x$ terms: $= 2x^2 + (2b + 3)x + 3b$.
3.  比較常數項 (Constant term)：$3b = -12 \Rightarrow b = -4$。
    Compare the constant term: $3b = -12 \Rightarrow b = -4$.
4.  比較 $x$ 項係數 (Coefficient of x)：$a = 2b + 3$。
    Compare the coefficient of x: $a = 2b + 3$.
5.  代入 $b$ 求 $a$：$a = 2(-4) + 3 = -8 + 3 = -5$。
    Substitute $b$ to find $a$: $a = 2(-4) + 3 = -8 + 3 = -5$.
    **Answer:** $a = -5, b = -4$.

## 6. 常見錯誤與迷思 (Common Mistakes & Misconceptions)

1.  **誤用 SSA 全等 (Misusing SSA Congruence)**
    *   **錯誤樣子：** 看到兩邊和一個「非夾角」相等，就說是全等。
        **What it looks like:** Seeing two sides and a "non-included angle" equal, and claiming they are congruent.
    *   **為什麼錯：** 邊-邊-角 (SSA) 不能保證三角形形狀固定（除非是直角 RHS）。
        **Why it's wrong:** Side-Side-Angle (SSA) cannot guarantee the triangle's shape is fixed (unless it's a right angle, RHS).
    *   **檢查口訣：** 「SSA 是壞掉的性質，只有 RHS 才是對的。」
        **Check Mnemonic:** "SSA is a broken rule; only RHS is correct."

2.  **根號運算的加法錯誤 (Addition Error in Square Roots)**
    *   **錯誤樣子：** $\sqrt{2} + \sqrt{3} = \sqrt{5}$。
        **What it looks like:** $\sqrt{2} + \sqrt{3} = \sqrt{5}$.
    *   **為什麼錯：** 根號內的數字不能直接相加，就像 $x + y \neq xy$。
        **Why it's wrong:** Numbers inside square roots cannot be added directly, just like $x + y \neq xy$.
    *   **檢查方法：** 只有「同類方根」才能合併（例如 $2\sqrt{3} + 3\sqrt{3} = 5\sqrt{3}$）。
        **Check Method:** Only "like radicals" can be combined (e.g., $2\sqrt{3} + 3\sqrt{3} = 5\sqrt{3}$).

3.  **多邊形外角和隨邊數改變 (Exterior Angle Sum Changing with Sides)**
    *   **錯誤樣子：** 認為 10 邊形的外角和比 3 邊形大。
        **What it looks like:** Thinking the sum of exterior angles of a decagon is larger than that of a triangle.
    *   **為什麼錯：** 無論幾邊形，外角和永遠是 360 度（轉一圈回到原點）。
        **Why it's wrong:** No matter how many sides, the sum of exterior angles is always 360 degrees (turning a full circle back to the start).

## 7. 建議練習與自我檢核 (Practice Suggestions & Self-Check)

### **練習建議 (Practice Suggestions)**
1.  **先畫圖 (Draw first):** 幾何題如果不畫圖，絕對做不出來。題目沒給圖，自己畫草圖。
    **Draw first:** You absolutely cannot solve geometry problems without a diagram. If the problem doesn't give one, sketch it yourself.
2.  **倒著想 (Think backwards):** 題目求 $\overline{AB}$ $\rightarrow$ 找包含 $\overline{AB}$ 的三角形 $\rightarrow$ 證明全等 $\rightarrow$ 找已知條件。
    **Think backwards:** Problem asks for $\overline{AB}$ $\rightarrow$ Find a triangle containing $\overline{AB}$ $\rightarrow$ Prove congruence $\rightarrow$ Find given conditions.
3.  **每天 3 題證明 (3 Proofs a day):** 證明題是這次段考的大魔王，多練習寫「因為...所以...」的過程。
    **3 Proofs a day:** Proof problems are the "final boss" of this midterm; practice writing the "Because... Therefore..." process.
