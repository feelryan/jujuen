# 3. 關鍵觀念與必備公式（Key Concepts & Formulas）

### A. 有機化學反應 (Organic Chemical Reactions)

**定義與直覺理解：**  
有機化學常考兩個「製造」反應：做香味（酯化）和做肥皂（皂化）。  
Organic chemistry often tests two "manufacturing" reactions: making scents (esterification) and making soap (saponification).

**必背公式與符號意義：**  
1. **酯化反應 (Esterification):**  
   有機酸 + 醇 $\xrightarrow{\text{濃硫酸}}$ 酯 + 水  
   Organic Acid + Alcohol $\xrightarrow{\text{Conc. } H_2SO_4}$ Ester + Water  
   *注意：濃硫酸是催化劑（脫水劑）。*  
   *Note: Concentrated sulfuric acid is the catalyst (dehydrating agent).*

2. **皂化反應 (Saponification):**  
   油脂 + 鹼（氫氧化鈉） $\rightarrow$ 肥皂 + 甘油  
   Oil/Fat + Alkali (NaOH) $\rightarrow$ Soap + Glycerol  
   *注意：需加入飽和食鹽水進行「鹽析」分離。*  
   *Note: Saturated salt water is added for "salting out" separation.*

**何時會用到？**  
當題目問到「水果香味」、「濃硫酸脫水」或是「加入食鹽水後浮在上層的物質」時。  
When a question asks about "fruit scents," "dehydration by sulfuric acid," or "substances floating on top after adding salt water."

---

### B. 摩擦力 (Friction)

**定義與直覺理解：**  
摩擦力是阻止物體運動的力。推不動時，摩擦力等於你的推力（靜摩擦力）；動起來後，摩擦力是固定的（動摩擦力）。  
Friction is the force that opposes motion. When it doesn't move, friction equals your push (static friction); once moving, friction is constant (kinetic friction).

**必背觀念：**  
- **靜止時 (At rest):** 靜摩擦力 = 外力 ($f_s = F$)。  
  Static friction = Applied force.
- **最大靜摩擦力 (Max Static Friction):** 物體剛要移動瞬間的力，與「正向力」成正比。  
  The force at the instant the object is about to move, proportional to the "Normal Force."
- **運動時 (In motion):** 動摩擦力 < 最大靜摩擦力，且為定值。  
  Kinetic friction < Max static friction, and it is a constant value.

**何時會用到？**  
看到水平推木塊的圖表題，問你「推力 100gw 時木塊不動，摩擦力是多少？」  
When seeing a graph of pushing a block horizontally, asking "If the push is 100gw and the block doesn't move, what is the friction?"

---

### C. 浮力 (Buoyancy)

**定義與直覺理解：**  
物體在水中減輕的重量，就是浮力。浮力等於「排開液體的重量」。  
The weight an object loses in water is buoyancy. Buoyancy equals the "weight of the displaced liquid."

**必背公式：**  
$B = V_{\text{下}} \times D_{\text{液}}$  
浮力 = 物體液面下的體積 $\times$ 液體密度  
Buoyancy = Volume submerged $\times$ Density of liquid

- **浮體 (Floating body):** 浮力 = 物重 ($B = W$)。  
  Buoyancy = Object's Weight.
- **沉體 (Sinking body):** 浮力 < 物重 ($B < W$)，浮力 = 物重 - 支撐力。  
  Buoyancy < Object's Weight, Buoyancy = Weight - Normal Force.

**何時會用到？**  
題目問「冰塊融化後水位變化」、「船從河開到海」、「鐵塊與木塊誰受浮力大」。  
When questions ask about "water level change after ice melts," "a ship moving from river to sea," or "comparing buoyancy between iron and wood blocks."

# 4. 典型題型整理（Typical Question Patterns）

### 題型一：比熱與熱平衡計算 (Specific Heat & Thermal Equilibrium)

**題型說明：**  
給定兩個不同溫度、不同質量的物質混合，求末溫或散失熱量。  
Given two substances with different temperatures and masses mixed together, find the final temperature or heat loss.

**解題步驟：**  
1. **Step 1:** 寫出核心觀念：「高溫放出的熱 = 低溫吸收的熱」。  
   Write down the core concept: "Heat released by high temp = Heat absorbed by low temp."
2. **Step 2:** 代入公式 $m_1 \times s_1 \times (T_{\text{高}} - T_{\text{末}}) = m_2 \times s_2 \times (T_{\text{末}} - T_{\text{低}})$。  
   Substitute into the formula.
3. **Step 3:** 若有熱散失，將散失的熱加在「吸熱」那一邊（或是從放熱中扣除）。  
   If there is heat loss, add the lost heat to the "absorbing" side (or subtract from released heat).

**小提示：**  
畫一個簡單的溫度計圖，高溫在上下降，低溫在下上升，中間是平衡溫度。  
Draw a simple thermometer diagram; high temp goes down, low temp goes up, equilibrium is in the middle.

---

### 題型二：摩擦力大小判斷 (Judging Friction Magnitude)

**題型說明：**  
給一張「外力 vs. 摩擦力」的折線圖，問特定推力下的摩擦力大小。  
Given a "Applied Force vs. Friction" line graph, ask for the friction magnitude at a specific push force.

**解題步驟：**  
1. **Step 1:** 先看推力是否超過「最大靜摩擦力」（轉折點）。  
   First, check if the push force exceeds the "Max Static Friction" (the turning point).
2. **Step 2:** 若推力 < 最大值 $\rightarrow$ 靜止 $\rightarrow$ 摩擦力 = 推力。  
   If Push < Max $\rightarrow$ Static $\rightarrow$ Friction = Push.
3. **Step 3:** 若推力 > 最大值 $\rightarrow$ 運動中 $\rightarrow$ 摩擦力 = 動摩擦力（水平線數值）。  
   If Push > Max $\rightarrow$ Moving $\rightarrow$ Friction = Kinetic Friction (the horizontal line value).

**小提示：**  
千萬不要看到推力很大就以為摩擦力也很大，動起來後摩擦力是固定的！  
Never assume friction is huge just because the push is huge; once moving, friction is constant!

---

### 題型三：有機化合物的分類與性質 (Classification of Organic Compounds)

**題型說明：**  
給出分子式或結構，問它是烴、醇、酸還是酯？溶不溶於水？  
Given a molecular formula or structure, ask if it's a hydrocarbon, alcohol, acid, or ester? Is it water-soluble?

**解題步驟：**  
1. **Step 1:** 看元素組成。只有 C, H $\rightarrow$ 烴類（難溶於水）。  
   Check elemental composition. Only C, H $\rightarrow$ Hydrocarbons (insoluble in water).
2. **Step 2:** 看官能基。有 -OH $\rightarrow$ 醇；有 -COOH $\rightarrow$ 酸；有香味 $\rightarrow$ 酯。  
   Check functional groups. Has -OH $\rightarrow$ Alcohol; Has -COOH $\rightarrow$ Acid; Has scent $\rightarrow$ Ester.
3. **Step 3:** 判斷溶解度。醇與酸（小分子）易溶於水；烴與酯難溶於水。  
   Judge solubility. Alcohols and Acids (small molecules) are soluble; Hydrocarbons and Esters are insoluble.

# 5. 精選範例講解（Worked Examples）

### 範例 1：浮力比較 (Buoyancy Comparison)

**題目重述：**  
將體積相同的鐵球、木球、保麗龍球放入水中。鐵球沉底，木球浮出一半，保麗龍球浮出 90%。請問誰受的浮力最大？  
**Problem:** Iron ball, wood ball, and styrofoam ball of the same volume are placed in water. Iron sinks, wood floats half-out, styrofoam floats 90% out. Which has the largest buoyancy?

**解題思路：**  
直覺可能會想「鐵球最重所以浮力最大？」或是「浮起來的浮力比較大？」。正確方法是用阿基米德原理：看「排開水的體積」。  
Intuition might suggest "Iron is heaviest so max buoyancy?" or "Floating ones have more buoyancy?". The correct method is Archimedes' Principle: look at "Volume of displaced water."

**詳細解答步驟：**  
1. 公式：$B = V_{\text{下}} \times D_{\text{水}}$。  
   Formula: $B = V_{\text{submerged}} \times D_{\text{water}}$.
2. 鐵球沉底 $\rightarrow$ $V_{\text{下}} = V_{\text{球}}$（全部體積）。  
   Iron sinks $\rightarrow$ $V_{\text{submerged}} = V_{\text{ball}}$ (Full volume).
3. 木球浮一半 $\rightarrow$ $V_{\text{下}} = 0.5 V_{\text{球}}$。  
   Wood floats half $\rightarrow$ $V_{\text{submerged}} = 0.5 V_{\text{ball}}$.
4. 保麗龍球浮 90% $\rightarrow$ $V_{\text{下}} = 0.1 V_{\text{球}}$。  
   Styrofoam floats 90% $\rightarrow$ $V_{\text{submerged}} = 0.1 V_{\text{ball}}$.
5. 結論：鐵球排開水最多，浮力最大。  
   Conclusion: Iron ball displaces the most water, so it has the largest buoyancy.

---

### 範例 2：皂化反應 (Saponification)

**題目重述：**  
將油脂與氫氧化鈉共煮，加入酒精。反應後倒入飽和食鹽水。請問：酒精的功用？浮在上層的是什麼？  
**Problem:** Boil oil with sodium hydroxide, add alcohol. After reaction, pour into saturated salt water. Questions: Function of alcohol? What floats on top?

**解題思路：**  
這是一個經典實驗題。要記住「油水不互溶」所以需要媒介，以及「肥皂不溶於食鹽水」。  
This is a classic experiment. Remember "oil and water don't mix" so a medium is needed, and "soap is insoluble in salt water."

**詳細解答步驟：**  
1. **酒精功用：** 均勻溶解油脂與鹼性溶液（作為溶劑），讓反應加速。  
   **Function of alcohol:** Dissolve oil and alkaline solution uniformly (as a solvent) to accelerate the reaction.
2. **加入食鹽水：** 進行「鹽析」，使肥皂與甘油分離。  
   **Adding salt water:** Perform "salting out" to separate soap from glycerol.
3. **分層結果：**  
   - 上層：肥皂（密度小，難溶於食鹽水）。  
     Top layer: Soap (low density, insoluble in salt water).
   - 下層：甘油 + 食鹽水（甘油易溶於水）。  
     Bottom layer: Glycerol + Salt water (Glycerol is water-soluble).

# 6. 常見錯誤與迷思（Common Mistakes & Misconceptions）

1. **混淆質量數與原子量**  
   **Mistake:** Confusing Mass Number with Atomic Mass.  
   - **錯誤樣貌：** 以為週期表上的數字就是質量數。  
     Thinking the number on the periodic table is the mass number.
   - **修正：** 質量數 = 質子 + 中子（一定是整數）；原子量是平均值（通常有小數）。  
     Correction: Mass Number = Protons + Neutrons (must be integer); Atomic Mass is an average (usually decimal).

2. **誤以為「烴類」包含醇或酸**  
   **Mistake:** Thinking "Hydrocarbons" include alcohols or acids.  
   - **錯誤樣貌：** 看到乙醇 ($C_2H_5OH$) 以為它是烴類，因為有碳有氫。  
     Seeing Ethanol and thinking it's a hydrocarbon because it has C and H.
   - **修正：** 烴類定義是「只」含碳、氫。有氧就不是烴。  
     Correction: Hydrocarbons contain "only" Carbon and Hydrogen. If there's Oxygen, it's not a hydrocarbon.

3. **浮力誤區：沉得越深浮力越大**  
   **Mistake:** Buoyancy fallacy: The deeper it sinks, the greater the buoyancy.  
   - **錯誤樣貌：** 以為潛水艇在深海受到的浮力比在淺海大。  
     Thinking a submarine has more buoyancy in deep sea than in shallow sea.
   - **修正：** 只要完全沒入水中，$V_{\text{下}}$ 固定，浮力就固定，與深度無關（除非液體密度改變）。  
     Correction: As long as it's fully submerged, $V_{\text{submerged}}$ is constant, so buoyancy is constant, regardless of depth.

4. **摩擦力方向判斷錯誤**  
   **Mistake:** Misjudging the direction of friction.  
   - **錯誤樣貌：** 走路時，腳底的摩擦力向後。  
     Thinking when walking, friction on the sole points backward.
   - **修正：** 腳向後蹬地，摩擦力給腳一個「向前」的反作用力，人才能前進。  
     Correction: The foot pushes backward on the ground, friction gives the foot a "forward" reaction force, enabling the person to move forward.
