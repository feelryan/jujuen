# 3. 關鍵觀念與必備公式（Key Concepts & Formulas）

### 1. 垂徑定理 (Perpendicular Chord Theorem)
*   **定義：** 圓心對弦作垂線，會平分這條弦。
    **Definition:** A line drawn from the center perpendicular to a chord bisects the chord.
*   **直覺理解：** 想像把圓沿著那條垂線對摺，左右兩邊會重合。
    **Intuitive Understanding:** Imagine folding the circle along that perpendicular line; the left and right sides will overlap.
*   **必備公式：** 直角三角形 $r^2 = d^2 + (\frac{L}{2})^2$
    **Formula:** Right triangle $r^2 = d^2 + (\frac{L}{2})^2$
    *   ($r$=半徑 radius, $d$=弦心距 chord distance, $L$=弦長 chord length)
*   **何時用到？** 題目給了「弦長」求「距離」，或是給「距離」求「半徑」。
    **When to use?** When the problem gives "chord length" to find "distance," or gives "distance" to find "radius."

### 2. 圓心角與圓周角 (Central Angle & Inscribed Angle)
*   **定義：** 對應同一段弧，圓周角是圓心角的一半。
    **Definition:** Subtending the same arc, the inscribed angle is half of the central angle.
*   **必備公式：** $\angle \text{Inscribed} = \frac{1}{2} \times \angle \text{Central} = \frac{1}{2} \times \text{Arc Degree}$
    **Formula:** $\angle \text{Inscribed} = \frac{1}{2} \times \angle \text{Central} = \frac{1}{2} \times \text{Arc Degree}$
*   **重要推論：** 直徑所對的圓周角必為 90 度（直角）。
    **Key Inference:** The inscribed angle subtended by a diameter is always 90 degrees (right angle).

### 3. 切線性質 (Tangent Properties)
*   **定義：** 圓外一點對圓作切線，切線與半徑垂直。
    **Definition:** A tangent line from an external point is perpendicular to the radius.
*   **必備公式：**
    1. $\angle \text{Radius-Tangent} = 90^\circ$
    2. 圓外一點到切點距離相等 (Two tangent segments from an external point are equal in length).
*   **何時用到？** 看到「切線」兩個字，馬上把半徑畫出來，標上直角。
    **When to use?** As soon as you see the word "tangent," draw the radius and mark the right angle.

### 4. 圓內接四邊形 (Cyclic Quadrilateral)
*   **定義：** 四個頂點都在圓上的四邊形。
    **Definition:** A quadrilateral whose four vertices all lie on a circle.
*   **必備公式：** 對角互補（相加等於 180 度）。
    **Formula:** Opposite angles are supplementary (sum up to 180 degrees).
    *   $\angle A + \angle C = 180^\circ$, $\angle B + \angle D = 180^\circ$

# 4. 典型題型整理（Typical Question Patterns）

### 題型一：斷掉的弦（求弦長或半徑）
**Pattern 1: The Broken Chord (Finding chord length or radius)**

*   **題型說明：** 給你半徑和弦心距，問你弦有多長。
    **Description:** Given the radius and the distance from the center to the chord, asks for the length of the chord.
*   **解題步驟 (Steps):**
    1. **畫圖：** 畫出圓心、弦、弦心距（垂直線）和半徑。
       **Draw:** Draw the center, the chord, the chord distance (perpendicular line), and the radius.
    2. **找直角三角形：** 利用半徑、弦心距、半弦長構成直角三角形。
       **Find Right Triangle:** Use the radius, chord distance, and half-chord length to form a right triangle.
    3. **畢氏定理：** $a^2 + b^2 = c^2$ 計算未知邊。
       **Pythagoras:** Calculate the unknown side using $a^2 + b^2 = c^2$.
    4. **注意：** 算出的是「半弦長」，記得乘以 2 才是答案。
       **Note:** What you calculated is "half the chord length"; remember to multiply by 2 for the final answer.

### 題型二：冰淇淋甜筒（切線長性質）
**Pattern 2: The Ice Cream Cone (Tangent Length Properties)**

*   **題型說明：** 圓外一點 $P$ 向圓畫兩條切線 $PA, PB$。
    **Description:** From a point $P$ outside the circle, draw two tangents $PA$ and $PB$.
*   **解題步驟 (Steps):**
    1. **標示等長：** 馬上寫下 $PA = PB$。
       **Mark Equality:** Immediately write down $PA = PB$.
    2. **連線圓心：** 連接 $OA, OB, OP$。
       **Connect Center:** Connect $OA, OB, OP$.
    3. **利用全等：** $\triangle OAP \cong \triangle OBP$ (RHS 全等)，所以角度也被平分。
       **Use Congruence:** $\triangle OAP \cong \triangle OBP$ (RHS congruence), so the angles are also bisected.
    4. **四邊形內角和：** 四邊形 $OAPB$ 中，$\angle A$ 和 $\angle B$ 是 90 度，所以 $\angle O + \angle P = 180^\circ$。
       **Quadrilateral Sum:** In quadrilateral $OAPB$, $\angle A$ and $\angle B$ are 90 degrees, so $\angle O + \angle P = 180^\circ$.

### 題型三：兩圓公切線（梯形變矩形）
**Pattern 3: Common Tangents of Two Circles (Trapezoid to Rectangle)**

*   **題型說明：** 兩個分開的圓，求外公切線長度。
    **Description:** Two separated circles; find the length of the external common tangent.
*   **解題步驟 (Steps):**
    1. **畫連心線：** 連接兩圓圓心 $O_1, O_2$。
       **Draw Line of Centers:** Connect the two centers $O_1, O_2$.
    2. **畫半徑：** 畫出兩條垂直於切線的半徑 $r_1, r_2$。
       **Draw Radii:** Draw the two radii $r_1, r_2$ perpendicular to the tangent.
    3. **平移切線：** 在內部畫一條平行線，構造出一個「長方形」和一個「直角三角形」。
       **Translate Tangent:** Draw a parallel line inside to construct a "rectangle" and a "right triangle."
    4. **計算：** 直角三角形的斜邊是連心線，一股是半徑差 $(r_1 - r_2)$，另一股就是切線長。
       **Calculate:** The hypotenuse is the distance between centers, one leg is the difference of radii $(r_1 - r_2)$, and the other leg is the tangent length.

# 5. 精選範例講解（Worked Examples）

### 範例 1：弦與弦心距 (Chord and Distance)
**題目：** 一圓的半徑為 10，圓心到一弦的距離為 6，求此弦的長度？
**Problem:** The radius of a circle is 10, and the distance from the center to a chord is 6. Find the length of this chord.

*   **直覺思路 (Intuition):**
    這是一個典型的「垂徑定理」題目。看到「距離」就要想到「垂直」，看到垂直就要想到「畢氏定理」。
    This is a classic "Perpendicular Chord Theorem" problem. Seeing "distance" implies "perpendicular," and seeing perpendicular implies "Pythagorean Theorem."

*   **詳細解答 (Solution):**
    1.  設圓心為 $O$，弦為 $AB$，弦的中點為 $M$。
        Let the center be $O$, the chord be $AB$, and the midpoint of the chord be $M$.
    2.  根據題目，$OM = 6$ (弦心距)，$OA = 10$ (半徑)。
        According to the problem, $OM = 6$ (chord distance), $OA = 10$ (radius).
    3.  在直角三角形 $\triangle OMA$ 中，使用畢氏定理：
        In the right triangle $\triangle OMA$, use the Pythagorean theorem:
        $AM^2 + OM^2 = OA^2$
        $AM^2 + 6^2 = 10^2$
    4.  計算 $AM$：
        Calculate $AM$:
        $AM^2 = 100 - 36 = 64$
        $AM = 8$
    5.  弦長 $AB$ 是 $AM$ 的兩倍：
        The chord length $AB$ is twice $AM$:
        $AB = 2 \times 8 = 16$
    6.  **答案：16**
        **Answer: 16**

### 範例 2：圓外切四邊形 (Tangential Quadrilateral)
**題目：** 四邊形 $ABCD$ 的四邊都與圓 $O$ 相切 (圓外切四邊形)。若 $AB = 10, CD = 8, AD = 6$，求 $BC$ 的長度？
**Problem:** All four sides of quadrilateral $ABCD$ are tangent to circle $O$ (Tangential Quadrilateral). If $AB = 10, CD = 8, AD = 6$, find the length of $BC$.

*   **直覺思路 (Intuition):**
    圓外切四邊形有一個超級好用的口訣：「上加下等於左加右」。這是因為切線長相等的性質互相抵消了。
    Tangential quadrilaterals have a super useful mnemonic: "Top plus Bottom equals Left plus Right." This is because the equal tangent lengths cancel each other out.

*   **詳細解答 (Solution):**
    1.  觀念：圓外切四邊形的「對邊和相等」。
        Concept: The sums of opposite sides of a tangential quadrilateral are equal.
    2.  列式：
        Set up the equation:
        $AB + CD = AD + BC$
    3.  代入數字：
        Substitute the numbers:
        $10 + 8 = 6 + BC$
    4.  計算：
        Calculate:
        $18 = 6 + BC$
        $BC = 12$
    5.  **答案：12**
        **Answer: 12**

# 6. 常見錯誤與迷思（Common Mistakes & Misconceptions）

1.  **混淆「弧度數」與「弧長」**
    **Confusing "Arc Degree" with "Arc Length"**
    *   *錯誤樣子：* 題目問弧長，你卻回答 60 度。
        *Mistake:* The question asks for arc length, but you answer 60 degrees.
    *   *修正：* 度數是角度 ($\circ$)，長度是公分 ($cm$)。長度公式是 $2\pi r \times \frac{\text{degree}}{360}$。
        *Correction:* Degree is an angle ($\circ$), length is in cm. The length formula is $2\pi r \times \frac{\text{degree}}{360}$.

2.  **忘記圓周角是圓心角的「一半」**
    **Forgetting that the Inscribed Angle is "Half" of the Central Angle**
    *   *錯誤樣子：* 看到弧是 80 度，就說對應的圓周角也是 80 度。
        *Mistake:* Seeing an arc of 80 degrees and saying the corresponding inscribed angle is also 80 degrees.
    *   *修正：* 只有「圓心」角才等於弧度數，「圓周」角要在牆壁上，比較擠，所以只有一半 (40 度)。
        *Correction:* Only the "Central" angle equals the arc degree. The "Inscribed" angle is on the wall, it's tighter, so it's only half (40 degrees).

3.  **計算連心線時，弄錯加減**
    **Mixing up addition and subtraction when calculating the line of centers**
    *   *錯誤樣子：* 兩圓「內切」，卻把半徑相加。
        *Mistake:* Two circles are "internally tangent," but you add the radii.
    *   *口訣：* 外切相加 (像兩個朋友手牽手)，內切相減 (像大肚能容，吞進去)。
        *Mnemonic:* Externally tangent adds (like two friends holding hands); Internally tangent subtracts (like a big belly containing the small one).

4.  **誤以為所有四邊形都有外接圓**
    **Mistakenly thinking all quadrilaterals have a circumcircle**
    *   *錯誤樣子：* 看到任意四邊形就直接用「對角互補」。
        *Mistake:* Seeing any quadrilateral and immediately using "opposite angles are supplementary."
    *   *修正：* 必須確認題目說「四點共圓」或是「圓內接四邊形」才能用。
        *Correction:* You must confirm the problem says "concyclic points" or "cyclic quadrilateral" before using it.

# 7. 建議練習與自我檢核（Practice Suggestions & Self-Check）

### 練習建議 (Practice Suggestions)
1.  **先畫圖：** 幾何題不畫圖，神仙也難救。即使題目有圖，也在旁邊自己畫一遍輔助線。
    **Draw first:** Geometry without drawings is impossible even for a genius. Even if the problem has a diagram, draw the auxiliary lines yourself next to it.
2.  **分類刷題：** 先做 10 題「求弦長」，再做 10 題「求角度」，最後做「兩圓位置」。不要跳著做。
    **Categorized drills:** Do 10 "Find Chord Length" problems first, then 10 "Find Angle" problems, and finally "Two Circle Positions." Don't jump around.
3.  **遮住答案：** 範例題先把解答遮住，自己算得出來才算會。
    **Cover the answer:** For example problems, cover the solution first. You only truly know it if you can solve it yourself.
