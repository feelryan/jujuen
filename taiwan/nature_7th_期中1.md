# 學習目標（Learning Objectives）

1.  **掌握科學方法與顯微鏡操作技巧**  
    Master the scientific method and microscope operation techniques.  
    能夠區分操作變因、控制變因與應變變因，並理解顯微鏡下影像的移動與放大原理。  
    Be able to distinguish between independent, control, and dependent variables, and understand the principles of image movement and magnification under a microscope.

2.  **理解細胞構造與物質進出機制**  
    Understand cell structure and the mechanisms of material transport.  
    能比較動植物細胞的差異（如細胞壁、葉綠體），並解釋擴散作用與滲透作用對細胞的影響。  
    Be able to compare the differences between plant and animal cells (e.g., cell wall, chloroplasts) and explain the effects of diffusion and osmosis on cells.

3.  **分辨細胞分裂與減數分裂的差異**  
    Distinguish the differences between cell division (mitosis) and meiosis.  
    清楚染色體數目的變化（2N vs. N）以及這兩種分裂方式發生的時機與目的。  
    Clearly understand the changes in chromosome number (2N vs. N) and the timing and purpose of these two types of division.

4.  **運用遺傳法則解決孟德爾題型**  
    Apply genetic laws to solve Mendelian problems.  
    能利用棋盤方格法（Punnett Square）推算基因型（如 Aa）與表現型（如高莖）的機率。  
    Be able to use the Punnett Square to calculate the probability of genotypes (e.g., Aa) and phenotypes (e.g., tall stems).

# 關鍵觀念與必備公式（Key Concepts & Formulas）

## 1. 顯微鏡的成像與操作 (Microscope Imaging & Operation)

*   **定義與直覺理解：**  
    複式顯微鏡看到的影像是「上下顛倒、左右相反」的。  
    The image seen through a compound microscope is "upside down and reversed left to right."  
    想像你把一張寫著字的紙旋轉 180 度，那就是你看到的樣子。  
    Imagine rotating a piece of paper with writing on it by 180 degrees; that is what you see.

*   **必背公式與符號意義：**  
    放大倍率 = 目鏡倍率 × 物鏡倍率  
    Magnification = Eyepiece Magnification × Objective Magnification  
    視野亮度：放大倍率越高，視野越暗，範圍越小。  
    Field Brightness: The higher the magnification, the darker the field of view and the smaller the range.

*   **何時會用到這個觀念？**  
    當題目問你「玻片往右移，影像往哪跑？」或是「換成高倍鏡後，細胞變大還是變小？」時。  
    When a question asks, "If the slide moves right, where does the image go?" or "After switching to high power, do cells get bigger or smaller?"

## 2. 變因的控制 (Variables in Experiments)

*   **定義與直覺理解：**  
    做實驗就像比賽，為了公平，只能有一個條件不同。  
    Doing an experiment is like a competition; to be fair, only one condition can be different.

*   **關鍵術語：**  
    *   **操作變因 (Independent Variable)：** 唯一改變的條件（例如：有無加水）。  
        The only condition that changes (e.g., with or without water).
    *   **控制變因 (Control Variable)：** 必須保持一致的條件（例如：溫度、光照）。  
        Conditions that must remain consistent (e.g., temperature, light).
    *   **應變變因 (Dependent Variable)：** 實驗的結果（例如：黴菌生長的數量）。  
        The result of the experiment (e.g., the amount of mold growth).

## 3. 擴散與滲透作用 (Diffusion & Osmosis)

*   **定義與直覺理解：**  
    物質喜歡從「人多」的地方擠向「人少」的地方，這就是擴散。  
    Substances like to squeeze from "crowded" places to "less crowded" places; this is diffusion.  
    如果是「水」通過細胞膜去平衡濃度，就叫做滲透。  
    If "water" passes through the cell membrane to balance concentration, it is called osmosis.

*   **口訣：**  
    水往「高濃度（鹽多糖多）」的地方跑。  
    Water runs towards "high concentration (more salt/sugar)" areas.

## 4. 細胞分裂 vs. 減數分裂 (Mitosis vs. Meiosis)

*   **定義與直覺理解：**  
    *   **細胞分裂 (Mitosis)：** 為了長大或修補，複製出一模一樣的自己（2N → 2N）。  
        For growth or repair, copying an exact replica of itself (2N → 2N).
    *   **減數分裂 (Meiosis)：** 為了生小孩，染色體減半，精子卵子結合後才會剛好（2N → N）。  
        For reproduction, chromosomes are halved so that they are just right after sperm and egg combine (2N → N).

*   **必背數據：**  
    *   細胞分裂：1 次分裂，產生 2 個子細胞，染色體數目不變。  
        Mitosis: 1 division, produces 2 daughter cells, chromosome number remains unchanged.
    *   減數分裂：2 次分裂，產生 4 個子細胞，染色體數目減半。  
        Meiosis: 2 divisions, produces 4 daughter cells, chromosome number is halved.

# 典型題型整理（Typical Question Patterns）

## 題型一：顯微鏡的移動與倍率 (Microscope Movement & Magnification)

1.  **題型說明：**  
    題目會給一個偏掉的影像，問你如何把它移回中央，或者問倍率改變後的視野變化。  
    The question will show an off-center image and ask how to move it back to the center, or ask about field changes after magnification changes.

2.  **解題步驟 (Step-by-Step)：**  
    *   **Step 1:** 判斷移動方向。口訣：「像在哪，玻片就往哪移」。  
        Determine the direction of movement. Mnemonic: "Move the slide towards where the image is."  
        (因為影像是倒的，你想把影像拉回來，實際上玻片要推過去)。  
        (Because the image is inverted, if you want to pull the image back, you actually have to push the slide there).
    *   **Step 2:** 判斷倍率影響。倍率變大 = 細胞變大 = 數目變少 = 視野變暗。  
        Determine the effect of magnification. Higher magnification = Larger cells = Fewer cells = Darker field of view.

3.  **小提示：**  
    如果是「解剖顯微鏡」，影像是正的，移動方向就和直覺一樣（想往右就移右）。  
    If it is a "dissecting microscope," the image is upright, and the movement direction is intuitive (move right to go right).

## 題型二：紅血球與植物細胞的滲透 (Osmosis in Cells)

1.  **題型說明：**  
    把細胞丟到清水或濃食鹽水中，問你細胞會變怎樣（破裂、萎縮或不變）。  
    Putting cells into fresh water or concentrated salt water and asking what happens to the cell (burst, shrink, or stay the same).

2.  **解題步驟 (Step-by-Step)：**  
    *   **Step 1:** 找出哪邊比較「鹹/濃」。  
        Identify which side is "saltier/more concentrated."
    *   **Step 2:** 水會流向「濃」的那一邊。  
        Water flows towards the "concentrated" side.
    *   **Step 3:**  
        *   若水流進細胞：動物細胞會脹破，植物細胞有細胞壁支撐不會破。  
            If water flows into the cell: Animal cells will burst, plant cells are supported by the cell wall and won't burst.
        *   若水流出細胞：細胞會萎縮（植物細胞膜會與細胞壁分離）。  
            If water flows out of the cell: The cell will shrink (plant cell membrane will separate from the cell wall).

## 題型三：孟德爾遺傳機率計算 (Mendelian Genetics Probability)

1.  **題型說明：**  
    給親代的基因型（如 Tt x tt），問子代出現某種性狀的機率。  
    Given the parents' genotypes (e.g., Tt x tt), ask for the probability of a certain trait appearing in the offspring.

2.  **解題步驟 (Step-by-Step)：**  
    *   **Step 1:** 畫出九宮格（棋盤方格）。  
        Draw a Punnett Square.
    *   **Step 2:** 把爸媽的基因填在上方和左方。  
        Fill in the parents' genes on the top and left.
    *   **Step 3:** 填滿四個格子，計算比例。  
        Fill in the four boxes and calculate the ratio.

# 精選範例講解（Worked Examples）

## 範例 1：顯微鏡操作 (Microscope Operation)

**題目重述：**  
小明用複式顯微鏡觀察，發現視野中的「e」字偏在**右上方**。若要將「e」移到視野正中央，他該將玻片往哪個方向移動？  
**Problem:** Xiao Ming observes an "e" using a compound microscope and finds it positioned in the **top right**. To move the "e" to the center of the field, in which direction should he move the slide?

**解題思路：**  
*   **直覺想法：** 東西在右上方，我應該把它往左下方拉回來？（這是錯的！）  
    *Intuitive thought:* The object is at the top right, so I should pull it back to the bottom left? (This is wrong!)
*   **正確觀念：** 複式顯微鏡影像是顛倒的。影像在右上方，代表實物其實在左下方。我們要追著影像跑。  
    *Correct Concept:* The compound microscope image is inverted. The image is at the top right, meaning the actual object is at the bottom left. We need to chase the image.
*   **口訣：** 「像在哪，就往哪移」。  
    *Mnemonic:* "Move towards where the image is."

**詳細解答步驟：**  
1.  影像在右上方 (Image is at top right).  
2.  根據口訣，玻片就往**右上方**推。  
    According to the mnemonic, push the slide towards the **top right**.  
3.  **答案：往右上方移動。**  
    **Answer: Move towards the top right.**

## 範例 2：遺傳機率 (Genetics Probability)

**題目重述：**  
豌豆的高莖 (T) 對矮莖 (t) 為顯性。若將兩株**高莖**豌豆雜交，子代中竟然出現了**矮莖**。請問親代的基因型組合為何？子代出現矮莖的機率是多少？  
**Problem:** Tall stem (T) is dominant over short stem (t) in peas. If two **tall** pea plants are crossed and a **short** stem appears in the offspring, what are the genotypes of the parents? What is the probability of having short stem offspring?

**解題思路：**  
*   **關鍵線索：** 爸媽都是顯性（高莖），卻生出隱性（矮莖）。  
    *Key Clue:* Both parents are dominant (tall), but they produced a recessive (short) offspring.
*   **推論：** 隱性性狀 (tt) 必須兩個基因都為 t，一個來自爸爸，一個來自媽媽。  
    *Inference:* The recessive trait (tt) requires both genes to be t, one from the father and one from the mother.
*   **結論：** 爸媽雖然是高莖，但都一定帶有一個 t。所以爸媽都是 Tt。  
    *Conclusion:* Although the parents are tall, they must both carry a t. Therefore, both parents are Tt.

**詳細解答步驟：**  
1.  列出算式：Tt × Tt。  
    Set up the cross: Tt × Tt.
2.  畫棋盤方格：  
    Draw the Punnett Square:
    *   TT (高/Tall)
    *   Tt (高/Tall)
    *   Tt (高/Tall)
    *   tt (矮/Short)
3.  四個格子中，只有一個是 tt。  
    Out of four boxes, only one is tt.
4.  **答案：親代為 Tt × Tt，矮莖機率為 1/4 (25%)。**  
    **Answer: Parents are Tt × Tt, probability of short stem is 1/4 (25%).**

# 常見錯誤與迷思（Common Mistakes & Misconceptions）

1.  **誤認為「顯性」基因比較強或比較多**  
    **Mistake:** Thinking "Dominant" genes are stronger or more common.  
    *   **糾正：** 顯性只是代表「有它就會表現出來」，不代表在自然界中數量最多（例如多指症是顯性，但很少見）。  
        *Correction:* Dominant only means "it will be expressed if present," not that it is most abundant in nature (e.g., Polydactyly is dominant but rare).

2.  **搞混「細胞質」與「細胞質液」**  
    **Mistake:** Confusing "Cytoplasm" with "Cytosol".  
    *   **糾正：** 七年級通常統稱「細胞質」，它是膠狀物質，許多化學反應（代謝）都在這裡發生，而不僅僅是填滿空間。  
        *Correction:* In 7th grade, it's usually collectively called "Cytoplasm." It is a jelly-like substance where many chemical reactions (metabolism) occur, not just filling space.

3.  **忘記植物細胞放入清水不會破**  
    **Mistake:** Forgetting that plant cells won't burst in fresh water.  
    *   **糾正：** 動物細胞會破（因為只有細胞膜）；植物細胞有「細胞壁」像硬殼一樣保護，所以只會膨脹，不會破。  
        *Correction:* Animal cells will burst (only cell membrane); plant cells have a "cell wall" acting like a hard shell for protection, so they only swell but don't burst.

4.  **減數分裂的染色體數目算錯**  
    **Mistake:** Miscalculating chromosome numbers in meiosis.  
    *   **檢查方法：** 看到「精子」、「卵子」或「花粉」，染色體數目直接「除以 2」。  
        *Check Method:* Whenever you see "sperm," "egg," or "pollen," divide the chromosome number directly by 2.
