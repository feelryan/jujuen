*   **單元 D：浩瀚宇宙 (The Vast Universe)**

# 3. 關鍵觀念與必備公式（Key Concepts & Formulas）

### 1. 功與功率 (Work and Power)
*   **定義**：施力使物體在「力的方向上」產生位移。
    **Definition**: Applying a force to cause displacement in the "direction of the force".
*   **公式**：
    **Formula**:
    *   功 (Work): $W = F \times S$ （單位：焦耳 Joule, J）
    *   功率 (Power): $P = \frac{W}{t} = \frac{F \times S}{t} = F \times v$ （單位：瓦特 Watt, W）
*   **常見情境**：手提重物水平走路（作功為 0，因為力與位移垂直）；爬樓梯（抵抗重力作功）。
    **Common Scenario**: Carrying a heavy object walking horizontally (Work is 0 because force is perpendicular to displacement); Climbing stairs (Work done against gravity).

### 2. 力矩與槓桿原理 (Torque and Lever Principle)
*   **定義**：力矩是讓物體轉動的能力。
    **Definition**: Torque is the ability to cause an object to rotate.
*   **公式**：
    **Formula**:
    *   力矩 (Torque): $\tau = F \times d_{\perp}$ （力 $\times$ 力臂）
    *   平衡條件 (Equilibrium): 順時針力矩和 = 逆時針力矩和 ($ \Sigma \tau_{cw} = \Sigma \tau_{ccw} $)
*   **常見情境**：蹺蹺板、用扳手轉螺絲（力臂越長越省力）。
    **Common Scenario**: Seesaws, turning a screw with a wrench (longer lever arm means less effort).

### 3. 歐姆定律與電阻 (Ohm's Law and Resistance)
*   **定義**：導體兩端的電壓與通過的電流成正比。
    **Definition**: The voltage across a conductor is directly proportional to the current flowing through it.
*   **公式**：
    **Formula**:
    *   $V = I \times R$
    *   電阻與幾何關係 (Resistance & Geometry): $R \propto \frac{L}{A}$ （長度越長電阻越大，截面積越粗電阻越小）。
*   **常見情境**：計算電路中某個燈泡的電流，或比較不同粗細導線的電阻。
    **Common Scenario**: Calculating the current of a specific bulb in a circuit, or comparing the resistance of wires with different thicknesses.

### 4. 地震規模與震度 (Earthquake Magnitude vs. Intensity)
*   **定義**：
    **Definition**:
    *   規模 (Magnitude): 代表釋放能量的大小，一次地震只有一個數值（小數點後一位）。
        Magnitude: Represents the amount of energy released; one earthquake has only one value (to one decimal place).
    *   震度 (Intensity): 代表地面搖晃的程度，各地不同（整數分級，0~7級）。
        Intensity: Represents the degree of ground shaking; varies by location (integer scale, 0-7 levels).

# 4. 典型題型整理（Typical Question Patterns）

### 題型一：電路串並聯的燈泡亮度與電流比較
**Type 1: Comparing bulb brightness and current in Series/Parallel circuits**

*   **題型說明**：題目會給出幾個燈泡或電阻的連接圖，問你哪顆最亮，或是電流大小關係。
    **Description**: The question gives a diagram of connected bulbs or resistors and asks which is brightest or the relationship between their currents.
*   **解題步驟 (Steps)**：
    1.  **辨認串並聯**：電流只有一條路是串聯，有分岔是並聯。
        **Identify Series/Parallel**: If current has only one path, it's series; if it splits, it's parallel.
    2.  **運用口訣**：串聯電流相同 ($I$ same)，並聯電壓相同 ($V$ same)。
        **Use the Mnemonic**: In series, Current is the same; in parallel, Voltage is the same.
    3.  **代入公式**：亮度看功率 ($P=IV$ 或 $P=I^2R$ 或 $P=V^2/R$)。
        **Apply Formula**: Brightness depends on Power.
*   **小提示**：若燈泡規格相同（電阻 $R$ 一樣），串聯時分到的電壓變少（變暗），並聯時電壓不變（亮度不變）。
    **Tip**: If bulbs are identical (same $R$), in series they get less voltage (dimmer); in parallel, voltage stays the same (brightness unchanged).

### 題型二：地層剖面圖的事件排序
**Type 2: Ordering events in Geological Cross-sections**

*   **題型說明**：給一張地質圖，上面有岩層、斷層、岩脈、侵蝕面，問發生的先後順序。
    **Description**: A geological map shows rock layers, faults, dikes, and erosion surfaces; asks for the chronological order.
*   **解題步驟 (Steps)**：
    1.  **疊加定律**：下面的岩層通常比上面的老（除非地層倒轉）。
        **Law of Superposition**: Lower layers are usually older than upper layers (unless overturned).
    2.  **截切定律**：切過別人的比較年輕（後來的才能切斷原本存在的）。
        **Law of Cross-cutting Relationships**: The one that cuts through is younger (only subsequent events can cut through existing ones).
    3.  **找侵蝕面**：波浪狀的線條代表曾經隆起、侵蝕、再沈積，代表時間的中斷。
        **Find Erosion Surfaces**: Wavy lines indicate uplift, erosion, and re-deposition, representing a gap in time.

### 題型三：日地月相對位置（月相與潮汐）
**Type 3: Relative positions of Sun-Earth-Moon (Moon Phases & Tides)**

*   **題型說明**：給出太陽光方向，問某個位置的月相，或是何時是大潮/小潮。
    **Description**: Given the direction of sunlight, asks for the moon phase at a specific position, or when spring/neap tides occur.
*   **解題步驟 (Steps)**：
    1.  **定太陽**：先確認陽光從哪邊來。
        **Locate the Sun**: First confirm where the sunlight is coming from.
    2.  **畫月球**：月球面向太陽的一半永遠是亮的。
        **Draw the Moon**: The half of the Moon facing the Sun is always lit.
    3.  **看角度**：從地球（中心）看過去，看到亮面的比例決定月相（全亮=滿月，背對=新月）。
        **Check the Angle**: Looking from Earth (center), the proportion of the lit side visible determines the phase (Fully lit = Full Moon, Back facing = New Moon).
    4.  **連線判斷潮汐**：三者成一直線是大潮（引力加成），成直角是小潮（引力抵銷）。
        **Connect lines for Tides**: Three in a line means Spring Tide (gravity adds up); Right angle means Neap Tide (gravity cancels out).

# 5. 精選範例講解（Worked Examples）

### 範例 1：導體電阻與幾何形狀的關係 (Physics - Resistance)
**Example 1: Relationship between Conductor Resistance and Geometry**

*   **題目重述**：
    有甲、乙、丙三條材質相同的導線。
    甲：長度 $2L$，截面積 $A$。
    乙：長度 $L$，截面積 $A$。
    丙：長度 $L$，截面積 $2A$。
    若接上相同電壓，請問流過三者的電流大小關係？
    **Problem Restatement**:
    There are three wires, A, B, and C, made of the same material.
    A: Length $2L$, Area $A$.
    B: Length $L$, Area $A$.
    C: Length $L$, Area $2A$.
    If connected to the same voltage, what is the relationship between the currents flowing through them?

*   **解題思路 (Thought Process)**：
    *   **直覺**：把導線想像成「走廊」。走廊越長越難走（電阻大），走廊越寬越好走（電阻小）。
    *   **Intuition**: Imagine the wire as a "hallway". Longer hallways are harder to walk through (high resistance), wider hallways are easier (low resistance).
    *   **公式**：$R \propto \frac{L}{A}$ （電阻正比於 長度/截面積）。
    *   **Formula**: $R \propto \frac{L}{A}$ (Resistance is proportional to Length/Area).

*   **詳細解答 (Detailed Solution)**：
    1.  **計算電阻比例 (Calculate Resistance Ratios)**:
        *   $R_{甲} \propto \frac{2L}{A} = 2$
        *   $R_{乙} \propto \frac{L}{A} = 1$
        *   $R_{丙} \propto \frac{L}{2A} = 0.5$
        *   電阻大小：甲 > 乙 > 丙 (Resistance: A > B > C)
    2.  **推導電流關係 (Derive Current Relationship)**:
        *   根據歐姆定律 $I = \frac{V}{R}$，電壓 $V$ 固定時，電阻越**大**，電流越**小**。
        *   According to Ohm's Law $I = \frac{V}{R}$, when Voltage $V$ is fixed, higher Resistance means lower Current.
    3.  **結論 (Conclusion)**:
        *   因為 $R_{甲} > R_{乙} > R_{丙}$，所以 $I_{丙} > I_{乙} > I_{甲}$。
        *   Since $R_A > R_B > R_C$, therefore $I_C > I_B > I_A$.

### 範例 2：單擺的能量轉換 (Physics - Energy Conservation)
**Example 2: Energy Conversion in a Pendulum**

*   **題目重述**：
    一個單擺從高點 A 靜止釋放，擺盪到最低點 B，再往上到 C。若不計空氣阻力，請問哪一點的「重力位能」最小？哪一點的「動能」最大？
    **Problem Restatement**:
    A pendulum is released from rest at high point A, swings to the lowest point B, and goes up to C. Ignoring air resistance, at which point is the "Gravitational Potential Energy" the lowest? At which point is the "Kinetic Energy" the highest?

*   **解題思路 (Thought Process)**：
    *   **直覺**：位能看高度，動能看速度。滑滑梯時，最高的時候最慢，最低的時候衝最快。
    *   **Intuition**: Potential energy depends on height; Kinetic energy depends on speed. Like a slide, you are slowest at the top and fastest at the bottom.
    *   **守恆律**：力學能守恆（位能＋動能＝定值）。
    *   **Conservation Law**: Conservation of Mechanical Energy (Potential + Kinetic = Constant).

*   **詳細解答 (Detailed Solution)**：
    1.  **分析位能 (Analyze Potential Energy)**:
        *   位能 $U = mgh$。高度 $h$ 最低的地方是 B 點。
        *   Potential Energy $U = mgh$. The point with the lowest height $h$ is point B.
        *   所以 B 點位能最小。 (So, point B has the lowest potential energy.)
    2.  **分析動能 (Analyze Kinetic Energy)**:
        *   能量守恆：位能減少的量 ＝ 動能增加的量。
        *   Energy Conservation: Loss in Potential Energy = Gain in Kinetic Energy.
        *   既然 B 點位能最小（全部都換出去了），那它的動能一定最大。
        *   Since point B has the lowest potential energy (all converted), its kinetic energy must be the highest.

# 6. 常見錯誤與迷思 (Common Mistakes & Misconceptions)

1.  **誤以為「作圓周運動」不需要力或不作功**
    **Misconception: Thinking "Circular Motion" requires no force or does no work.**
    *   **錯誤想法**：轉圈圈速度不變，所以沒有受力？
        *Wrong Idea*: Speed is constant in rotation, so no force?
    *   **修正**：速度的「方向」一直在變，所以有加速度，需要「向心力」。但向心力與位移垂直，所以向心力**不作功**（功=0）。
        *Correction*: The "direction" of speed changes, so there is acceleration requiring "Centripetal Force". But centripetal force is perpendicular to displacement, so it does **NO work** (Work=0).

2.  **混淆地震規模 (Magnitude) 與震度 (Intensity)**
    **Misconception: Confusing Earthquake Magnitude and Intensity.**
    *   **錯誤想法**：台北震度 4 級，高雄震度 2 級，所以台北的地震規模比較大？
        *Wrong Idea*: Taipei has Intensity 4, Kaohsiung has Intensity 2, so the Magnitude in Taipei is larger?
    *   **修正**：**規模只有一個**（代表那顆原子彈多大），全台灣收到的規模數值都一樣。震度則是看你離震央多遠、搖多大。
        *Correction*: **Magnitude is singular** (how big the bomb was); the magnitude value is the same everywhere. Intensity depends on how far you are and how much it shakes.

3.  **串聯電路中，誤以為後面的燈泡比較暗**
    **Misconception: In a series circuit, thinking the bulb at the end is dimmer.**
    *   **錯誤想法**：電流流過第一個燈泡被消耗了，流到第二個就變少了？
        *Wrong Idea*: Current gets consumed by the first bulb, so less flows to the second?
    *   **修正**：串聯電路中，**電流處處相等**。就像水管，流進去多少水，流出來就多少水。
        *Correction*: In a series circuit, **current is the same everywhere**. Like a water pipe, water in equals water out.

4.  **月相盈虧的方向搞反**
    **Misconception: Reversing the direction of Moon phases (Waxing/Waning).**
    *   **口訣**：光從右邊來（太陽在右），月亮就先亮右邊（農曆上半月，上弦月）。
        *Mnemonic*: If light comes from the right (Sun on the right), the right side of the Moon lights up first (First half of lunar month, First Quarter).
    *   **檢查**：農曆十五（滿月）以前是亮西邊（右邊），十五以後亮東邊（左邊）。
        *Check*: Before the 15th (Full Moon), the West (Right) is lit; after the 15th, the East (Left) is lit.
