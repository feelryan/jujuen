# 3. 關鍵觀念與必備公式 (Key Concepts & Formulas)

## 3.1 一元一次方程式的運算 (Operations of Linear Equations)

*   **定義與直覺理解**：
    把方程式想像成一個「天平」，等號兩邊要保持平衡。我們要把 $x$ 留在左邊，數字趕到右邊。
    **Definition and Intuition**:
    Imagine the equation as a "balance scale"; both sides of the equal sign must remain balanced. We want to keep $x$ on the left and move numbers to the right.

*   **必背公式與符號意義**：
    *   **移項法則 (Transposition Rule)**：加變減，減變加；乘變除，除變乘。
        **Transposition Rule**: Plus becomes minus, minus becomes plus; multiply becomes divide, divide becomes multiply.
    *   **去括號 (Removing Parentheses)**：分配律 $a(b+c) = ab + ac$。注意負號！$-(b+c) = -b -c$。
        **Removing Parentheses**: Distributive property $a(b+c) = ab + ac$. Watch the negative sign! $-(b+c) = -b -c$.

*   **何時會用到？**
    算年齡（爸爸幾歲、兒子幾歲）、算錢（打折後多少錢）。
    **When to use?**
    Calculating age (how old is dad, how old is the son), calculating money (how much after discount).

## 3.2 正比與反比 (Direct and Inverse Proportion)

*   **定義與直覺理解**：
    *   **正比**：你變大，我也跟著變大（倍數相同）。例：買越多杯珍奶，總價越高。
        **Direct Proportion**: As you increase, I increase too (same multiple). Ex: The more bubble tea you buy, the higher the total price.
    *   **反比**：你變大，我就變小（乘積固定）。例：越多人一起做家事，花的時間越少。
        **Inverse Proportion**: As you increase, I decrease (fixed product). Ex: The more people doing chores together, the less time it takes.

*   **必背公式與符號意義**：
    *   **正比 (Direct)**：$y = kx$ （$y$ 除以 $x$ 是定值，$y/x = k$）。
        **Direct**: $y = kx$ ($y$ divided by $x$ is a constant, $y/x = k$).
    *   **反比 (Inverse)**：$xy = k$ （$x$ 乘以 $y$ 是定值）。
        **Inverse**: $xy = k$ ($x$ multiplied by $y$ is a constant).

## 3.3 一元一次不等式 (Linear Inequalities)

*   **定義與直覺理解**：
    解法跟方程式幾乎一樣，只有一個「超級陷阱」：同乘除負數時，開口方向要變！
    **Definition and Intuition**:
    The solution method is almost the same as equations, with one "super trap": when multiplying or dividing by a negative number, the direction of the inequality sign must flip!

*   **符號意義**：
    *   $\ge$ (不小於/至少/以上)；$\le$ (不大於/至多/以下/不超過)。
    *   $\ge$ (not less than / at least / or more); $\le$ (not greater than / at most / or less / does not exceed).

*   **必背口訣**：
    除以負數，符號轉向。
    **Must-memorize mnemonic**:
    Divide by a negative, flip the sign.

## 3.4 統計數據 (Statistical Data)

*   **定義與直覺理解**：
    *   **平均數 (Mean)**：全部加起來除以總個數（代表整體水準）。
        **Mean**: Sum of all values divided by the total count (represents overall level).
    *   **中位數 (Median)**：**先由小排到大**，站在最中間的那個數。
        **Median**: **Sort from small to large first**, then take the number standing right in the middle.
    *   **眾數 (Mode)**：出現次數最多的數。
        **Mode**: The number that appears most frequently.

# 4. 典型題型整理 (Typical Question Patterns)

## 題型一：去括號與化簡 (Simplifying Expressions with Parentheses)
*   **題型說明**：題目會給一長串包含 $x$ 和括號的式子，問你化簡後的結果。
    **Description**: The problem gives a long expression containing $x$ and parentheses, asking for the simplified result.
*   **解題步驟 (Steps)**：
    1.  **拆括號**：利用分配律，將括號前的數字乘進去。**注意：如果是減號，括號內全部變號！**
        **Remove Parentheses**: Use the distributive property to multiply the number in. **Note: If there is a minus sign, change all signs inside!**
    2.  **同類項合併**：有 $x$ 的跟有 $x$ 的加減，純數字跟純數字加減。
        **Combine Like Terms**: Add/subtract $x$ terms together, and constant terms together.

## 題型二：折扣與優惠問題 (Discount and Offer Problems)
*   **題型說明**：常見於不等式應用題，例如「買幾張票以上打折比較划算」。
    **Description**: Common in inequality word problems, such as "Buying more than how many tickets makes the discount worthwhile."
*   **小提示**：
    有時候「買比較多張票（雖然沒去那麼多人）」反而比較便宜，因為折扣門檻到了。
    **Tip**:
    Sometimes "buying more tickets (even if not that many people go)" is actually cheaper because the discount threshold is reached.

## 題型三：速度與追趕問題 (Speed and Catch-up Problems)
*   **題型說明**：兩個人跑馬拉松或開車，速率不同，問何時追上或距離多少。
    **Description**: Two people running a marathon or driving at different speeds; asks when they catch up or what the distance is.
*   **必備公式**：距離 = 速率 $\times$ 時間。
    **Formula**: Distance = Speed $\times$ Time.
*   **解題關鍵**：通常「距離」是固定的，或者兩人走的「時間」有差異。
    **Key**: Usually the "distance" is fixed, or there is a difference in the "time" traveled by the two.

# 5. 精選範例講解 (Worked Examples)

## 範例 1：不等式應用——門票優惠 (Inequality Application - Ticket Discount)

**題目重述**：
某遊樂園門票每張 800 元。若購買 50 張以上打 85 折，購買 100 張以上打 6 折。
如果一個團體超過 50 人但不足 100 人，請問至少要有多少人，直接買 100 張票反而比依實際人數買票還便宜？
**Problem Restatement**:
An amusement park ticket costs 800 yuan. Buying 50+ tickets gets 15% off (85% price), and buying 100+ tickets gets 40% off (60% price).
If a group has more than 50 but fewer than 100 people, what is the minimum number of people where buying 100 tickets directly is cheaper than buying for the actual number of people?

**解題思路 (Thinking Process)**：
*   直覺想法：買 100 張雖然浪費了一些票，但因為打 6 折（便宜很多），總價可能更低。
    **Intuition**: Buying 100 tickets wastes some, but because of the 40% discount (much cheaper), the total price might be lower.
*   我們要比較： 「$x$ 人用 85 折買」 vs 「直接買 100 張用 6 折買」。
    We need to compare: "$x$ people buying at 85% price" vs. "Buying 100 tickets directly at 60% price."

**詳細解答 (Detailed Solution)**：

1.  設團體人數為 $x$ 人。
    Let the number of people in the group be $x$.
2.  列出不等式：(實際人數花費) $>$ (買 100 張的花費)。
    Set up the inequality: (Cost for actual people) $>$ (Cost for 100 tickets).
    $$ 800 \times x \times 0.85 > 800 \times 100 \times 0.60 $$
3.  兩邊同時除以 800 (消掉票價，數字變小好算)。
    Divide both sides by 800 (cancel out ticket price, simpler numbers).
    $$ 0.85x > 60 $$
4.  計算 $x$：
    Calculate $x$:
    $$ x > \frac{60}{0.85} = \frac{6000}{85} \approx 70.58 $$
5.  因為人數必須是整數，且要比 70.58 大，所以 $x$ 至少是 71。
    Since the number of people must be an integer and greater than 70.58, $x$ must be at least 71.

**答案**：71 人。
**Answer**: 71 people.

## 範例 2：代數化簡與錯誤分析 (Algebraic Simplification & Error Analysis)

**題目重述**：
化簡 $\frac{2x+1}{3} - \frac{x-2}{2}$。
**Problem Restatement**:
Simplify $\frac{2x+1}{3} - \frac{x-2}{2}$.

**解題思路 (Thinking Process)**：
*   這是分數減法，分母不同（3 和 2），第一步一定要**通分**。
    This is fraction subtraction with different denominators (3 and 2); the first step must be **finding a common denominator**.
*   **超級易錯點**：中間的減號會影響後面分子的正負號！
    **Super Common Mistake**: The minus sign in the middle affects the sign of the numerator following it!

**詳細解答 (Detailed Solution)**：

1.  通分，分母變為 6。
    Find common denominator, which becomes 6.
    $$ \frac{2(2x+1)}{6} - \frac{3(x-2)}{6} $$
2.  合併為一個分數（**注意：要把分子括號起來**）。
    Combine into one fraction (**Note: Keep numerators in parentheses**).
    $$ \frac{2(2x+1) - 3(x-2)}{6} $$
3.  去括號（分配律）。
    Remove parentheses (Distributive property).
    $$ \frac{(4x + 2) - (3x - 6)}{6} $$
    *(注意：$-3$ 乘以 $-2$ 變成 $+6$)*
    *(Note: $-3$ multiplied by $-2$ becomes $+6$)*
4.  合併同類項。
    Combine like terms.
    $$ \frac{4x - 3x + 2 + 6}{6} = \frac{x + 8}{6} $$

**答案**：$\frac{x+8}{6}$ (或 $\frac{1}{6}x + \frac{4}{3}$)。
**Answer**: $\frac{x+8}{6}$ (or $\frac{1}{6}x + \frac{4}{3}$).

# 6. 常見錯誤與迷思 (Common Mistakes & Misconceptions)

1.  **去括號時忘記變號 (Forgetting to change signs when removing parentheses)**
    *   **錯誤樣子**：$3 - (2x + 5) = 3 - 2x + 5$。
    *   **Error Look**: $3 - (2x + 5) = 3 - 2x + 5$.
    *   **為什麼錯**：減號是對整個括號作用，$+5$ 也要變成減的。
    *   **Why it's wrong**: The minus sign applies to the whole parenthesis; $+5$ must also become subtracted.
    *   **正確**：$3 - 2x - 5$。
    *   **Correct**: $3 - 2x - 5$.

2.  **解不等式忘記換方向 (Forgetting to flip the inequality sign)**
    *   **錯誤樣子**：$-2x > 6 \implies x > -3$。
    *   **Error Look**: $-2x > 6 \implies x > -3$.
    *   **為什麼錯**：同除以負數，大小關係會顛倒。
    *   **Why it's wrong**: Dividing by a negative number reverses the size relationship.
    *   **正確**：$x < -3$。
    *   **Correct**: $x < -3$.

3.  **正反比搞混 (Confusing Direct and Inverse Proportion)**
    *   **錯誤樣子**：題目說「$x, y$ 成反比」，學生寫 $y = kx$。
    *   **Error Look**: Problem says "$x, y$ are inversely proportional," student writes $y = kx$.
    *   **檢查方法**：
        *   正比是「除法固定」($y/x=k$)。
        *   Direct is "division is constant" ($y/x=k$).
        *   反比是「乘法固定」($xy=k$)。
        *   Inverse is "multiplication is constant" ($xy=k$).

4.  **算中位數忘記排序 (Forgetting to sort for Median)**
    *   **錯誤樣子**：資料是 $5, 1, 3$，直接選中間的 $1$ 當中位數。
    *   **Error Look**: Data is $5, 1, 3$, directly choosing the middle $1$ as median.
    *   **正確**：一定要先排成 $1, 3, 5$，中位數是 $3$。
    *   **Correct**: Must sort to $1, 3, 5$ first; the median is $3$.
