## 3. 關鍵觀念與必備公式 (Key Concepts & Formulas)

### A. 不等式的關鍵規則 (Key Rules of Inequalities)

**觀念：負數運算要變號**
**Concept: Flip the sign when operating with negative numbers.**

*   **定義：** 當不等式兩邊同時「乘以」或「除以」一個**負數**時，大於要變小於，小於要變大於。
    **Definition:** When both sides of an inequality are "multiplied" or "divided" by a **negative number**, greater than becomes less than, and less than becomes greater than.
*   **直覺理解：** 想像你在數線上，乘以負號就像把世界「左右顛倒」了，所以方向也要跟著顛倒。
    **Intuitive Understanding:** Imagine you are on a number line; multiplying by a negative sign is like "flipping the world left and right," so the direction must also flip.
*   **必背口訣：** 同乘除負數，開口要反向。
    **Memorization Tip:** When multiplying or dividing by a negative, reverse the opening.
*   **常見陷阱：** 移項加減不用變號，只有乘除負數才要！
    **Common Trap:** Moving terms by addition or subtraction does NOT require changing the sign; only multiplying or dividing by a negative does!

### B. 統計三大天王 (The Big Three of Statistics)

**1. 算術平均數 (Mean)**
*   **公式：** 所有資料總和 ÷ 資料個數。
    **Formula:** Sum of all data ÷ Number of data points.
*   **意義：** 大家把東西集中起來，重新「平分」。
    **Meaning:** Everyone gathers their items and "shares them equally" again.

**2. 中位數 (Median)**
*   **定義：** 資料由小到大排列後，位於「最中間」的那個數。
    **Definition:** The number located in the "exact middle" after arranging data from smallest to largest.
*   **公式：**
    *   若資料有 $N$ 個（奇數）：第 $\frac{N+1}{2}$ 個數。
        If there are $N$ data points (odd): The $\frac{N+1}{2}$-th number.
    *   若資料有 $N$ 個（偶數）：第 $\frac{N}{2}$ 個與第 $\frac{N}{2}+1$ 個的平均。
        If there are $N$ data points (even): The average of the $\frac{N}{2}$-th and the $(\frac{N}{2}+1)$-th numbers.

**3. 眾數 (Mode)**
*   **定義：** 出現次數最多的數。
    **Definition:** The number that appears most frequently.
*   **直覺理解：** 班上最高票當選的班長。
    **Intuitive Understanding:** The class monitor elected with the highest number of votes.

### C. 三視圖 (Orthographic Views)

**觀念：投影 (Projection)**
*   **定義：** 從正前方、正右方、正上方看過去，壓扁後的平面圖形。
    **Definition:** The flattened 2D shapes seen from the front, right, and top.
*   **技巧：** 想像你用手電筒照這個物體，牆上的影子就是視圖。
    **Tip:** Imagine shining a flashlight on the object; the shadow on the wall is the view.

## 4. 典型題型整理 (Typical Question Patterns)

從這幾份試卷中，我整理出最常出現的三種題型：
From these exam papers, I have summarized the three most common question types:

### Type 1: 預算與門票問題 (Budget and Ticket Problems)
*   **題型說明：** 已知總預算或兩種收費方案，問買多少才夠，或哪種方案划算。
    **Description:** Given a total budget or two pricing plans, ask how much to buy to be sufficient, or which plan is more cost-effective.
*   **解題步驟 (Steps):**
    1.  **設未知數：** 設買了 $x$ 個物品或人數為 $x$。
        **Set variable:** Let the number of items bought be $x$ or the number of people be $x$.
    2.  **列出不等式：** 總花費 $\le$ 預算；或是 方案A花費 $<$ 方案B花費。
        **Set up inequality:** Total Cost $\le$ Budget; or Cost of Plan A $<$ Cost of Plan B.
    3.  **解 $x$ 並取整數：** 算出 $x$ 的範圍，根據題目問「至少」或「至多」找整數解。
        **Solve for $x$ and find integer:** Calculate the range of $x$, and find the integer solution based on whether the question asks for "at least" or "at most."

### Type 2: 找中位數在哪一組 (Finding the Median Group)
*   **題型說明：** 給你一個次數分配表（或長條圖），問中位數落在幾分到幾分之間。
    **Description:** Given a frequency distribution table (or bar chart), ask which score range the median falls into.
*   **解題步驟 (Steps):**
    1.  **算總人數 $N$：** 把所有次數加起來。
        **Calculate total count $N$:** Add up all frequencies.
    2.  **找中間位置：** 算出 $\frac{N+1}{2}$ 或 $\frac{N}{2}$。
        **Find the middle position:** Calculate $\frac{N+1}{2}$ or $\frac{N}{2}$.
    3.  **累加次數：** 從第一組開始把人數加起來，直到超過中間位置，那一組就是答案。
        **Accumulate frequencies:** Add up the counts starting from the first group until you exceed the middle position; that group is the answer.

### Type 3: 數積木與視圖 (Counting Blocks and Views)
*   **題型說明：** 給你一個立體圖，問用了幾個積木，或者選出正確的視圖。
    **Description:** Given a 3D figure, ask how many blocks are used, or select the correct view.
*   **小提示：** 在考卷上直接標數字！在上視圖的每一個格子裡，寫下那一疊有幾個積木。
    **Hint:** Write numbers directly on the exam paper! In each square of the top view, write down how many blocks are in that stack.

## 5. 精選範例講解 (Worked Examples)

### 範例一：打折優惠比較 (Example 1: Comparing Discounts)

**題目：**
遊樂園門票每張 500 元。團體票規定：30 人以上（含）打八折。
如果我們班不到 30 人，但我們買 30 張團體票反而比依實際人數買原價票還便宜。請問我們班至少有多少人？
**Problem:**
Amusement park tickets are 500 yuan each. Group ticket rule: 30 people or more (inclusive) get 20% off (pay 80%).
If our class has fewer than 30 people, but buying 30 group tickets is cheaper than buying regular tickets for the actual number of people. How many people are in our class at least?

**解題思路 (Thought Process):**
*   **直覺：** 雖然我們沒那麼多人，但假裝有 30 人去買打折票，居然比老實買票便宜？這表示我們人數其實很接近 30 了。
    **Intuition:** Even though we don't have that many people, pretending to be 30 people to buy discounted tickets is cheaper than buying honestly? This means our number is actually very close to 30.
*   **關鍵：** 「買30張的錢」 $<$ 「買 $x$ 張原價的錢」。
    **Key:** "Cost of 30 tickets" $<$ "Cost of $x$ regular tickets".

**詳細解答 (Solution):**
1.  設班上有 $x$ 人。
    Let there be $x$ people in the class.
2.  實際買票費用：$500 \times x = 500x$。
    Actual ticket cost: $500 \times x = 500x$.
3.  買 30 張團體票費用：$500 \times 30 \times 0.8$ (八折)。
    Cost of 30 group tickets: $500 \times 30 \times 0.8$ (20% off).
4.  列式：$500 \times 30 \times 0.8 < 500x$。
    Set up inequality: $500 \times 30 \times 0.8 < 500x$.
5.  兩邊同除以 500：$24 < x$。
    Divide both sides by 500: $24 < x$.
6.  因為 $x$ 要比 24 大，且題目問「至少」，整數解為 25。
    Since $x$ must be greater than 24, and the question asks for "at least," the integer solution is 25.
    
**答案：** 至少 25 人。
**Answer:** At least 25 people.

### 範例二：統計表中的中位數 (Example 2: Median in a Table)

**題目：**
某班 40 位學生體重次數分配表如下：
**Problem:**
The weight frequency distribution table for a class of 40 students is as follows:

| 體重 (Weight) | 40-50 | 50-60 | 60-70 | 70-80 |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| 人數 (Count) | 5 | 12 | 18 | 5 |

請問中位數落在哪一組？
Which group does the median fall into?

**解題思路 (Thought Process):**
*   **直覺：** 不要只看「哪一組人最多」，要找「排隊排在中間的那個人」。
    **Intuition:** Don't just look at "which group has the most people"; look for "the person standing in the middle of the line."
*   **計算：** 40 個人，中間是第 20 和第 21 個人。
    **Calculation:** For 40 people, the middle ones are the 20th and 21st persons.

**詳細解答 (Solution):**
1.  總人數 $N = 40$。
    Total number of people $N = 40$.
2.  中位數位置：第 $\frac{40}{2}=20$ 位 與 第 $21$ 位。
    Median position: The 20th and 21st person.
3.  開始累加 (Accumulate):
    *   40-50組：5人 (累積 5人，還沒到 20)。
        40-50 group: 5 people (Accumulated 5, not yet 20).
    *   50-60組：12人 (累積 $5+12=17$人，還沒到 20)。
        50-60 group: 12 people (Accumulated $5+12=17$, not yet 20).
    *   60-70組：18人 (累積 $17+18=35$人，超過 20 和 21 了！)。
        60-70 group: 18 people (Accumulated $17+18=35$, exceeded 20 and 21!).
4.  所以第 20 和 21 人都在 60-70 這一組。
    Therefore, both the 20th and 21st persons are in the 60-70 group.

**答案：** 60-70 公斤。
**Answer:** 60-70 kg.

## 6. 常見錯誤與迷思 (Common Mistakes & Misconceptions)

在改考卷時，老師最常看到這些錯誤，請務必小心：
When grading exams, teachers see these mistakes most often; please be careful:

**錯誤 1：不等式乘除負數忘記變號**
**Mistake 1: Forgetting to flip the sign when multiplying/dividing inequalities by a negative number.**
*   **錯誤樣子：** $-2x > 6 \Rightarrow x > -3$ (錯！Wrong!)
*   **正確做法：** $-2x > 6 \Rightarrow x < -3$ (對！Right!)
*   **檢查口訣：** 看到負號除過去，箭頭馬上轉頭。
    **Check Tip:** See a negative sign dividing over? Turn the arrow around immediately.

**錯誤 2：把「不小於」寫成「大於」**
**Mistake 2: Writing "not less than" as "greater than".**
*   **錯誤理解：** 「不小於」就是「大於」。
    **Misunderstanding:** "Not less than" means "greater than."
*   **正確觀念：** 「不小於」包含「等於」，所以是 $\ge$。
    **Correct Concept:** "Not less than" includes "equal to," so it is $\ge$.
*   **同理：** 「不超過」是 $\le$ (小於等於)。
    **Similarly:** "Not exceeding" is $\le$ (less than or equal to).

**錯誤 3：中位數找錯人**
**Mistake 3: Finding the wrong person for the median.**
*   **錯誤樣子：** 總共 9 人，中位數是第 $9 \div 2 = 4.5$，所以找第 4.5 人？
    **Error:** Total 9 people, median is $9 \div 2 = 4.5$, so look for the 4.5th person?
*   **正確做法：** 奇數個資料，公式是 $\frac{N+1}{2}$。$\frac{9+1}{2}=5$，是第 5 人。
    **Correct Method:** For odd number of data, the formula is $\frac{N+1}{2}$. $\frac{9+1}{2}=5$, so it's the 5th person.

## 7. 建議練習與自我檢核 (Practice Suggestions & Self-Check)

### 練習建議 (Practice Suggestions)
1.  **先畫圖：** 遇到不等式範圍題（如 $x > -3$），先在數線上畫出來，確認空心還是實心。
    **Draw first:** When facing inequality range questions (like $x > -3$), draw it on the number line first to confirm if the circle is open or closed.
2.  **再算表：** 找幾題統計圖表的題目，練習算出平均數和中位數，確認自己不會算錯。
    **Calculate tables:** Find a few statistics chart problems, practice calculating the mean and median to ensure you don't miscalculate.
3.  **最後想立體：** 拿幾個橡皮擦或積木堆疊，對照課本的三視圖，訓練大腦轉換視角。
    **Visualize 3D last:** Stack a few erasers or blocks, compare them with the orthographic views in the textbook, and train your brain to switch perspectives.
