## 3. 關鍵觀念與必備公式 (Key Concepts & Formulas)

### A. 絕對值與相反數 (Absolute Value & Opposites)
*   **定義 (Definition):** 絕對值 $|a|$ 是數線上 $a$ 到原點的距離；相反數是與原點距離相同但方向相反的數。
    The absolute value $|a|$ is the distance from $a$ to the origin on the number line; the opposite is the number with the same distance from the origin but in the opposite direction.
*   **直覺理解 (Intuition):** $|-5|$ 就是「我走了 5 步，不管方向」。相反數就是照鏡子，$-3$ 的鏡像是 $3$。
    $|-5|$ means "I walked 5 steps, regardless of direction". The opposite is like looking in a mirror; the reflection of $-3$ is $3$.
*   **必背公式 (Formula):**
    *   若 $|x| = 5$，則 $x = 5$ 或 $-5$。
        If $|x| = 5$, then $x = 5$ or $-5$.
    *   $a - b$ 的相反數是 $b - a$。
        The opposite of $a - b$ is $b - a$.

### B. 指數律 (Exponent Rules)
*   **定義 (Definition):** $a^n$ 代表 $n$ 個 $a$ 相乘。
    $a^n$ represents multiplying $a$ by itself $n$ times.
*   **陷阱 (Trap):**
    *   $(-2)^4 = (-2)(-2)(-2)(-2) = 16$ (有括號，負號乘4次)
        (With parentheses, the negative sign is multiplied 4 times)
    *   $-2^4 = -(2 \times 2 \times 2 \times 2) = -16$ (沒括號，先算次方再加負號)
        (Without parentheses, calculate the power first, then add the negative sign)

### C. 二元一次聯立方程式 (Systems of Linear Equations)
*   **定義 (Definition):** 兩個方程式，兩個未知數 ($x, y$)，求同時滿足兩者的解。
    Two equations, two unknowns ($x, y$), finding the solution that satisfies both simultaneously.
*   **何時使用 (When to use):** 當題目有兩個未知的量（例如：大人票價與小孩票價、雞的數量與兔的數量）。
    When a problem has two unknown quantities (e.g., adult ticket price vs. child ticket price, number of chickens vs. rabbits).

### D. 象限與坐標 (Quadrants & Coordinates)
*   **符號意義 (Symbol Meaning):** $P(x, y)$。$x$ 管左右，$y$ 管上下。
    $P(x, y)$. $x$ controls left/right, $y$ controls up/down.
*   **口訣 (Mnemonic):**
    *   第一象限 (Q1): $(+, +)$ 右上 (Top Right)
    *   第二象限 (Q2): $(-, +)$ 左上 (Top Left)
    *   第三象限 (Q3): $(-, -)$ 左下 (Bottom Left)
    *   第四象限 (Q4): $(+, -)$ 右下 (Bottom Right)
*   **距離公式 (Distance Formula):** 點 $P(a, b)$ 到 $x$ 軸距離是 $|b|$（看 $y$ 坐標的絕對值）。
    The distance from point $P(a, b)$ to the x-axis is $|b|$ (look at the absolute value of the y-coordinate).

## 4. 典型題型整理 (Typical Question Patterns)

### Type 1: 數線上的移動 (Movement on the Number Line)
*   **題型說明:** 蝸牛爬牆、螞蟻移動、溫度升降。
    **Description:** Snails climbing walls, ants moving, temperature changes.
*   **解題步驟 (Steps):**
    1.  **畫圖 (Draw):** 畫出一條數線，標出起點。
        Draw a number line and mark the starting point.
    2.  **判斷方向 (Direction):** 向右/向上是「加」，向左/向下是「減」。
        Right/Up is "plus", Left/Down is "minus".
    3.  **列式計算 (Calculate):** 終點 = 起點 + 移動量。
        End Point = Start Point + Movement.
*   **小提示 (Tip):** 注意「相差」永遠是用大數減小數。
    Note that "difference" is always calculated as the larger number minus the smaller number.

### Type 2: 應用題—買票與分組 (Word Problems - Tickets & Grouping)
*   **題型說明:** 已知總人數、總金額，求男生女生各幾人，或兩種票各買幾張。
    **Description:** Given total people and total cost, find the number of boys/girls or tickets of each type.
*   **解題步驟 (Steps):**
    1.  **設未知數 (Set Variables):** 設男生 $x$ 人，女生 $y$ 人。
        Let there be $x$ boys and $y$ girls.
    2.  **找兩個條件 (Find 2 Conditions):** 通常一個是「數量和」，一個是「總價/總分」。
        Usually one is "sum of quantities" and the other is "total price/score".
    3.  **列聯立方程式 (Set up System):**
        $\begin{cases} x + y = \text{總人數 (Total People)} \\ 30x + 20y = \text{總金額 (Total Cost)} \end{cases}$
    4.  **求解 (Solve):** 使用加減消去法。
        Use the elimination method.

### Type 3: 坐標幾何—圖形面積 (Coordinate Geometry - Area of Shapes)
*   **題型說明:** 給你三條直線方程式（例如 $x=0, y=0, 2x+3y=6$），求圍成的三角形面積。
    **Description:** Given three linear equations (e.g., $x=0, y=0, 2x+3y=6$), find the area of the enclosed triangle.
*   **解題步驟 (Steps):**
    1.  **找交點 (Find Intersections):** 分別找出直線與 $x$ 軸、 $y$ 軸的交點。
        Find the intersections of the line with the x-axis and y-axis respectively.
    2.  **畫草圖 (Sketch):** 標出這三點，確認是直角三角形。
        Mark these three points and confirm it is a right-angled triangle.
    3.  **算底高 (Base & Height):** 底和高通常就是截距的絕對值。
        The base and height are usually the absolute values of the intercepts.
    4.  **公式 (Formula):** 面積 = $\frac{1}{2} \times \text{底} \times \text{高}$。
        Area = $\frac{1}{2} \times \text{Base} \times \text{Height}$.

## 5. 精選範例講解 (Worked Examples)

### 範例一：整數運算與分配律 (Integer Operations & Distributive Property)
*(改編自中山國中試題 / Adapted from Zhongshan JH Exam)*

**題目 (Problem):**
計算 $3(-5x - 8y) - (4y - 3x)$ 並化簡。
Calculate $3(-5x - 8y) - (4y - 3x)$ and simplify.

**解題思路 (Thought Process):**
直覺上我們會想直接把數字乘進去，但最容易錯的地方是「後面的負號」。要把減號當成 $(-1)$ 乘進去括號裡的每一項。
Intuitively, we want to multiply the numbers in directly, but the most common mistake is the "negative sign at the back". Treat the minus sign as $(-1)$ multiplying into every term inside the parenthesis.

**解答步驟 (Solution Steps):**
1.  **展開第一個括號 (Expand first bracket):**
    $3 \times (-5x) = -15x$
    $3 \times (-8y) = -24y$
    $\rightarrow -15x - 24y$
2.  **展開第二個括號 (Expand second bracket):**
    $-(4y) = -4y$
    $-(-3x) = +3x$ (負負得正！ / Negative times negative is positive!)
    $\rightarrow -4y + 3x$
3.  **合併同類項 (Combine like terms):**
    $(-15x + 3x) + (-24y - 4y)$
    $= -12x - 28y$

**Answer:** $-12x - 28y$

---

### 範例二：聯立方程式應用 (System of Equations Application)
*(改編自仁愛國中試題 / Adapted from Renai JH Exam)*

**題目 (Problem):**
班上買了雞排和紅茶。雞排一份 50 元，紅茶一杯 20 元。已知買了 10 份雞排和 $y$ 杯紅茶，總共花了 700 元。請問紅茶買了幾杯？
The class bought chicken fillets and black tea. A chicken fillet costs 50 NTD, and a black tea costs 20 NTD. It is known that 10 chicken fillets and $y$ cups of tea were bought, costing 700 NTD in total. How many cups of tea were bought?

**解題思路 (Thought Process):**
這題其實只有一個未知數 $y$，但我們可以把它看作二元一次觀念的基礎。關鍵是「總價 = 單價 $\times$ 數量」。
This problem actually has only one unknown $y$, but we can view it as the foundation of two-variable concepts. The key is "Total Price = Unit Price $\times$ Quantity".

**解答步驟 (Solution Steps):**
1.  **列出算式 (Set up equation):**
    $(\text{雞排總價}) + (\text{紅茶總價}) = 700$
    $(\text{Total Chicken Price}) + (\text{Total Tea Price}) = 700$
    $50 \times 10 + 20 \times y = 700$
2.  **化簡 (Simplify):**
    $500 + 20y = 700$
3.  **移項求解 (Isolate and solve):**
    $20y = 700 - 500$
    $20y = 200$
    $y = 10$

**Answer:** 10 杯 (10 cups).

## 6. 常見錯誤與迷思 (Common Mistakes & Misconceptions)

1.  **去括號變號錯誤 (Sign Error in Expansion)**
    *   **錯誤 (Mistake):** $-(3x - 5) = -3x - 5$
    *   **原因 (Reason):** 忘記負號要分配給後面的 $-5$。
        Forgot to distribute the negative sign to the $-5$ at the back.
    *   **修正 (Correction):** $-(3x - 5) = -3x + 5$ (負負得正 / Negative negative positive).

2.  **絕對值當作括號 (Treating Absolute Value as Parentheses)**
    *   **錯誤 (Mistake):** $|-3 - 5| = 3 - 5 = -2$
    *   **原因 (Reason):** 絕對值必須先算出裡面的結果，最後再變正數。
        Absolute value requires calculating the result inside first, then making it positive.
    *   **修正 (Correction):** $|-3 - 5| = |-8| = 8$.

3.  **點到軸的距離搞混 (Confusing Distance to Axes)**
    *   **錯誤 (Mistake):** 點 $(3, -5)$ 到 $x$ 軸距離是 3。
    *   **原因 (Reason):** 到 $x$ 軸看 $y$ 坐標，到 $y$ 軸看 $x$ 坐標。
        To the x-axis, look at the y-coordinate; to the y-axis, look at the x-coordinate.
    *   **修正 (Correction):** 到 $x$ 軸距離是 $|-5| = 5$。
        Distance to x-axis is $|-5| = 5$.

4.  **科學記號的指數大小 (Magnitude of Scientific Notation)**
    *   **錯誤 (Mistake):** $3 \times 10^{-5}$ 比 $5 \times 10^{-6}$ 小，因為 $3 < 5$。
    *   **原因 (Reason):** 比較科學記號時，先比次方，次方大的數就大。
        When comparing scientific notation, compare the exponents first; the number with the larger exponent is larger.
    *   **修正 (Correction):** $-5 > -6$，所以 $3 \times 10^{-5}$ 比較大。
        $-5 > -6$, so $3 \times 10^{-5}$ is larger.

## 7. 建議練習與自我檢核 (Practice Suggestions & Self-Check)

### 練習建議 (Practice Suggestions)
*   **先做計算題 (Calculation First):** 確保去括號、正負號運算 100% 正確。這是拿分基本盤。
    Ensure expansion and sign operations are 100% correct. This is the baseline for scoring.
*   **畫圖輔助 (Draw Diagrams):** 遇到「數線移動」或「坐標距離」的題目，不要空想，畫個草圖立刻就懂。
    When facing "number line movement" or "coordinate distance" problems, don't just imagine it; draw a sketch to understand it immediately.
*   **檢查單位 (Check Units):** 應用題中，錢的單位（元）、時間（分/秒）是否一致？
    In word problems, are the units for money (dollars) or time (minutes/seconds) consistent?
