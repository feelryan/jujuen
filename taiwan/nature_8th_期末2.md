# 1. 學習目標（Learning Objectives）

*   **掌握反應速率與化學平衡的核心觀念**
    Master the core concepts of reaction rates and chemical equilibrium.
    能夠分辨溫度、濃度、表面積與催化劑如何影響反應快慢，並理解可逆反應的平衡移動。
    Be able to distinguish how temperature, concentration, surface area, and catalysts affect reaction speed, and understand the equilibrium shift in reversible reactions.

*   **熟悉有機化合物的分類與性質**
    Be familiar with the classification and properties of organic compounds.
    學會區分烴類、醇類、有機酸、酯類與聚合物，並記住常見物質（如乙醇、醋酸、肥皂）的特性。
    Learn to distinguish between hydrocarbons, alcohols, organic acids, esters, and polymers, and memorize the properties of common substances (such as ethanol, acetic acid, and soap).

*   **精通壓力與浮力的計算與受力分析**
    Master the calculation and force analysis of pressure and buoyancy.
    能夠運用 $P=h \times d$ 與阿基米德原理，解決液體壓力、大氣壓力與物體浮沉的應用題。
    Be able to apply $P=h \times d$ and Archimedes' principle to solve application problems involving liquid pressure, atmospheric pressure, and floating/sinking objects.

*   **釐清摩擦力的種類與計算**
    Clarify the types and calculations of friction.
    分辨靜摩擦力（可變）與最大靜摩擦力（固定）的差異，並了解影響摩擦力的因素。
    Distinguish the difference between static friction (variable) and maximum static friction (fixed), and understand the factors affecting friction.

    *   *Key points:* Archimedes' principle, judgment of floating and sinking bodies.

# 3. 關鍵觀念與必備公式（Key Concepts & Formulas）

## A. 液體壓力 (Liquid Pressure)

*   **定義與直覺理解：**
    液體壓力來自於上方液體的重量壓下來。
    Liquid pressure comes from the weight of the liquid pressing down from above.
    越深的地方，頭頂上的水越多，壓力就越大。
    The deeper you go, the more water is above your head, so the greater the pressure.

*   **必背公式與符號意義：**
    $P = h \times d$
    $P$：液體壓力 (Liquid Pressure, $gw/cm^2$)
    $h$：垂直深度 (Vertical Depth, $cm$) —— **注意是從液面往下算的垂直距離！** (Note: It is the vertical distance measured down from the surface!)
    $d$：液體密度 (Liquid Density, $g/cm^3$)

*   **何時會用到？**
    看到「連通管」、「水壩底部比較厚」、「深海魚」或「不同液體高度比較」的題目。
    When seeing questions about "communicating vessels," "dams being thicker at the bottom," "deep-sea fish," or "comparing heights of different liquids."

## B. 阿基米德原理 / 浮力 (Archimedes' Principle / Buoyancy)

*   **定義與直覺理解：**
    物體在水中減輕的重量，就是浮力。
    The weight an object loses in water is the buoyancy.
    浮力的大小，等於物體「擠走」的那些水的重量。
    The magnitude of buoyancy is equal to the weight of the water the object "pushes away."

*   **必背公式與符號意義：**
    $B = V_{\text{sub}} \times d_{\text{liq}}$
    $B$：浮力 (Buoyancy, $gw$)
    $V_{\text{sub}}$：物體滅入液體中的體積 (Volume submerged, $cm^3$)
    $d_{\text{liq}}$：液體密度 (Liquid Density, $g/cm^3$)

    **特別判斷 (Special Case)：**
    若物體是**浮體 (Floating body)**：浮力 = 物體總重量 ($B = W$)。
    If the object is a **floating body**: Buoyancy = Total weight of the object ($B = W$).

*   **何時會用到？**
    題目出現「船」、「冰塊浮在水面」、「潛水艇」或比較不同物體在水中的受力。
    When questions involve "ships," "ice cubes floating on water," "submarines," or comparing forces on different objects in water.

## C. 影響反應速率的因素 (Factors Affecting Reaction Rate)

*   **定義與直覺理解：**
    化學反應就像碰碰車，粒子必須「有效碰撞」才會發生反應。
    Chemical reactions are like bumper cars; particles must have "effective collisions" for a reaction to occur.
    任何能增加碰撞次數或能量的方法，都能加快反應。
    Any method that increases the frequency or energy of collisions can speed up the reaction.

*   **必背觀念：**
    1. **濃度 (Concentration)**：車子越多，越容易撞。 (More cars, easier to crash.)
    2. **表面積 (Surface Area)**：接觸面越大（磨成粉），撞擊機會越多。 (Larger contact area (ground into powder), more chances to crash.)
    3. **溫度 (Temperature)**：車速越快（能量高），撞得越猛烈。 (Faster speed (higher energy), harder crashes.)
    4. **催化劑 (Catalyst)**：降低門檻，讓反應更容易發生（不消耗本身）。 (Lowers the threshold, making the reaction easier to happen (does not consume itself).)

*   **何時會用到？**
    題目問「為什麼火藥要磨成粉」、「為什麼夏天食物易壞」或「雙氧水製氧加二氧化錳」。
    When questions ask "why gunpowder is ground into powder," "why food spoils easily in summer," or "adding manganese dioxide to hydrogen peroxide."

# 4. 典型題型整理（Typical Question Patterns）

## Type 1: 浮力比一比 (Comparing Buoyancy)

*   **題型說明：**
    題目會給幾個不同材質或大小的物體，丟進水中，問誰受的浮力最大？
    The question will give several objects of different materials or sizes, throw them into water, and ask which one receives the greatest buoyancy?

*   **解題步驟 (Solution Steps)：**
    1. **Step 1: 判斷浮沉 (Judge floating or sinking)**
       先看密度：密度小於水是浮體，密度大於水是沉體。
       Check density first: Density less than water means it floats; density greater than water means it sinks.
    2. **Step 2: 選用公式 (Choose the formula)**
       *   **浮體 (Floating)**：$B = W$ （浮力等於物重，不用管體積）。
           **Floating**: $B = W$ (Buoyancy equals object weight, ignore volume).
       *   **沉體 (Sinking)**：$B = V \times d_{\text{liq}}$ （浮力等於體積乘以液體密度）。
           **Sinking**: $B = V \times d_{\text{liq}}$ (Buoyancy equals volume times liquid density).
    3. **Step 3: 比較數值 (Compare values)**
       將算出的數值進行排序。
       Sort the calculated values.

*   **小提示 (Tip)：**
    看到「浮在水面」，直接想「浮力=重量」，不要去算排開多少水，那樣太慢了！
    When you see "floating on the surface," think "Buoyancy = Weight" immediately. Don't try to calculate how much water is displaced; that's too slow!

## Type 2: 托里切利實驗與大氣壓力 (Torricelli Experiment & Atmospheric Pressure)

*   **題型說明：**
    給出一張倒立水銀管的圖，問管內壓力、某點壓力，或是換成水會怎樣。
    Shows a diagram of an inverted mercury tube and asks about the pressure inside the tube, at a certain point, or what happens if changed to water.

*   **解題步驟 (Solution Steps)：**
    1. **Step 1: 找基準線 (Find the reference line)**
       液面上的大氣壓力 ($P_{\text{atm}}$) 支撐著管內的水銀柱。
       The atmospheric pressure ($P_{\text{atm}}$) on the liquid surface supports the mercury column inside the tube.
    2. **Step 2: 讀取垂直高度 (Read vertical height)**
       找管內液面與管外液面的「垂直高度差」($h$)。傾斜管子不影響垂直高度。
       Find the "vertical height difference" ($h$) between the liquid surface inside and outside the tube. Tilting the tube does not affect vertical height.
    3. **Step 3: 換算單位 (Convert units)**
       $1 \text{ atm} = 76 \text{ cmHg} = 1033.6 \text{ gw/cm}^2$。
       $1 \text{ atm} = 76 \text{ cmHg} = 1033.6 \text{ gw/cm}^2$.

*   **小提示 (Tip)：**
    如果管子上方不是真空（有氣體），管內的液柱會變低，因為裡面的氣體也在往下壓。
    If the top of the tube is not a vacuum (contains gas), the liquid column inside will be lower because the gas inside is also pushing down.

## Type 3: 有機化合物的鑑別 (Identifying Organic Compounds)

*   **題型說明：**
    給出甲、乙、丙、丁四種液體或分子模型，描述它們的特性（溶於水、導電、氣味），要你配對。
    Gives four liquids or molecular models (A, B, C, D), describes their properties (solubility, conductivity, smell), and asks you to match them.

*   **解題步驟 (Solution Steps)：**
    1. **Step 1: 看導電性 (Check conductivity)**
       有機酸（如醋酸）溶於水可導電；醇類、烴類、酯類通常不導電。
       Organic acids (like acetic acid) conduct electricity when dissolved in water; alcohols, hydrocarbons, and esters usually do not.
    2. **Step 2: 看溶解度 (Check solubility)**
       醇類（乙醇）與酸類（醋酸）易溶於水；烴類（油）與酯類難溶於水。
       Alcohols (ethanol) and acids (acetic acid) dissolve easily in water; hydrocarbons (oil) and esters dissolve poorly.
    3. **Step 3: 聞氣味 (Smell the odor)**
       酯類有香味（水果香）；醋酸有刺鼻酸味。
       Esters have a fragrance (fruity smell); acetic acid has a pungent sour smell.

# 5. 精選範例講解（Worked Examples）

## 範例 1：浮力與密度的綜合判斷 (Buoyancy and Density)

**題目 (Problem)：**
將質量相同、但材質不同的甲（保麗龍）、乙（木塊）、丙（鉛塊）三個物體投入水中。甲、乙浮在水面上，丙沉入水底。請問誰受到的浮力最大？
Three objects of the same mass but different materials, A (Styrofoam), B (Wood), and C (Lead), are thrown into water. A and B float on the surface, while C sinks to the bottom. Which one receives the greatest buoyancy?

**解題思路 (Thought Process)：**
*   *直覺想法：* 鉛塊最重感覺沉得最深，是不是浮力最大？（這是錯的！）
    *Intuitive thought:* The lead block feels the heaviest and sinks the deepest, so does it have the greatest buoyancy? (This is wrong!)
*   *正確思路：* 質量相同 ($M$ 固定)。浮體看重量，沉體看體積。
    *Correct thought:* Mass is the same ($M$ is fixed). Look at weight for floating bodies, look at volume for sinking bodies.

**詳細解答 (Detailed Solution)：**

1.  **分析甲與乙 (Analyze A and B)**：
    它們都是浮體 (Floating bodies)。
    They are both floating bodies.
    浮力公式：$B = W$ (浮力 = 物重)。
    Buoyancy formula: $B = W$ (Buoyancy = Object Weight).
    因為質量相同，所以 $B_{\text{甲}} = B_{\text{乙}} = W$。
    Since the mass is the same, $B_A = B_B = W$.

2.  **分析丙 (Analyze C)**：
    它是沉體 (Sinking body)。
    It is a sinking body.
    浮力公式：$B = V \times d_{\text{water}}$。
    Buoyancy formula: $B = V \times d_{\text{water}}$.
    因為丙是鉛塊，密度很大 ($d_{\text{鉛}} > d_{\text{水}}$)，所以它的體積很小 ($V = M/d$)。
    Since C is lead, its density is very high ($d_{\text{lead}} > d_{\text{water}}$), so its volume is very small ($V = M/d$).
    它排開的水重（浮力）會小於它自己的重量 ($B_{\text{丙}} < W$)。
    The weight of the water it displaces (buoyancy) will be less than its own weight ($B_C < W$).

3.  **結論 (Conclusion)**：
    甲 = 乙 > 丙。
    A = B > C.
    **答案：甲與乙浮力相等且最大。**
    **Answer: A and B have equal and maximum buoyancy.**

## 範例 2：反應速率與接觸面積 (Reaction Rate and Surface Area)

**題目 (Problem)：**
取質量相等的貝殼（碳酸鈣），分別以「整塊」與「磨成粉末」的狀態，加入同濃度、同體積的鹽酸中。請問哪一個產生二氧化碳的「總量」比較多？哪一個「產生氣泡的速率」比較快？
Take shells (calcium carbonate) of equal mass, in the state of "whole block" and "ground powder" respectively, and add them to hydrochloric acid of the same concentration and volume. Which one produces a greater "total amount" of carbon dioxide? Which one has a faster "rate of bubble production"?

**解題思路 (Thought Process)：**
*   *直覺想法：* 粉末反應很快，所以氣體應該比較多吧？
    *Intuitive thought:* Powder reacts very fast, so it should produce more gas, right?
*   *關鍵區分：* 「速率」是快慢，「總量」是產物多少。
    *Key distinction:* "Rate" is speed, "Total amount" is quantity of product.

**詳細解答 (Detailed Solution)：**

1.  **分析速率 (Analyze Rate)**：
    粉末的表面積大於整塊 (Surface area of powder > Whole block)。
    碰撞次數多，反應速率快。
    More collisions, faster reaction rate.
    **速率：粉末 > 整塊 (Rate: Powder > Block)。**

2.  **分析總量 (Analyze Total Amount)**：
    反應物的質量是固定的（貝殼質量相同，鹽酸也足量）。
    The mass of reactants is fixed (shell mass is the same, acid is sufficient).
    無論反應快慢，最後能生成的 $CO_2$ 總量由「限量試劑（貝殼）」決定。
    Regardless of speed, the final total amount of $CO_2$ generated is determined by the "limiting reagent (shell)."
    **總量：粉末 = 整塊 (Total Amount: Powder = Block)。**

3.  **結論 (Conclusion)**：
    粉末只是先跑完，但終點一樣遠。
    The powder just finishes first, but the finish line is at the same distance.

# 6. 常見錯誤與迷思（Common Mistakes & Misconceptions）

1.  **誤以為催化劑可以增加產量**
    *Mistake: Thinking catalysts can increase yield.*
    *說明：* 催化劑只能「縮短時間」，不能「變出更多產物」。就像開快車可以早點到家，但不能讓家變大。
    *Explanation:* Catalysts can only "shorten the time," not "produce more product." Just like driving fast gets you home sooner, but doesn't make your home bigger.
    *檢查：* 看到圖表時，檢查最終的高度是否改變。
    *Check:* When seeing a graph, check if the final height changes.

2.  **混淆「酸性」與「有機酸」**
    *Mistake: Confusing "Acidic" with "Organic Acid".*
    *說明：* 鹽酸 ($HCl$) 是酸性，但它是無機酸；醋酸 ($CH_3COOH$) 才是含有碳的有機酸。
    *Explanation:* Hydrochloric acid ($HCl$) is acidic, but it is an inorganic acid; Acetic acid ($CH_3COOH$) is the organic acid containing carbon.
    *口訣：* 有機酸一定要有 $-COOH$ 原子團。
    *Mnemonic:* Organic acids must have the $-COOH$ group.

3.  **計算液體壓力時用錯高度**
    *Mistake: Using the wrong height when calculating liquid pressure.*
    *說明：* 學生常拿容器的「斜邊長」或「離地面的高度」來算。
    *Explanation:* Students often use the "slant height" of the container or the "height from the ground" to calculate.
    *修正：* $h$ 永遠是指「從液面垂直往下」到該點的距離。
    *Correction:* $h$ always refers to the vertical distance "down from the liquid surface" to that point.

4.  **以為摩擦力是定值**
    *Mistake: Thinking friction is a constant value.*
    *說明：* 靜摩擦力會隨著推力改變。你推 10gw 不動，摩擦力就是 10gw；推 20gw 不動，摩擦力就是 20gw。只有在「動起來」之後（動摩擦力）或「正要動」（最大靜摩擦力）才是定值。
    *Explanation:* Static friction changes with the pushing force. If you push 10gw and it doesn't move, friction is 10gw; push 20gw and it doesn't move, friction is 20gw. Only after "starting to move" (kinetic friction) or "just about to move" (max static friction) is it a constant value.
